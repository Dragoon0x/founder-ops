# Reference: SaaS Benchmarks (current as of 2026)

## Stage-Based Benchmarks

### Pre-Seed
- ARR: $0 - $500K
- Growth: Doesn't matter; focus on retention
- Customers: 5-50
- Team: 2-5
- Burn: $20K-50K/mo
- Runway expected: 12-18 months

### Seed
- ARR: $500K - $2M
- Growth: 10-20% MoM
- Customers: 50-500
- Team: 5-15
- Burn: $50K-150K/mo
- Runway expected: 18-24 months

### Series A
- ARR: $2M - $8M
- Growth: 12-20% MoM (after $2M)
- Customers: 200-2000
- Team: 15-50
- Burn: $200K-500K/mo
- Runway expected: 18-24 months
- Net retention: 110%+

### Series B
- ARR: $8M - $30M
- Growth: 100%+ YoY
- Customers: 1K-10K
- Team: 50-150
- Burn: $500K-1.5M/mo
- Net retention: 115%+

### Series C+
- ARR: $30M+
- Growth: 60-100% YoY
- Customers: 5K+
- Team: 100-500
- Path to profitability visible

## Healthy Benchmarks

### Net Revenue Retention by Stage
- Seed: 90-100% (still figuring it out)
- Series A: 105-115%
- Series B: 110-130%
- Best in class: 130%+

### Churn (Annual)
- B2C: 30-50%
- SMB SaaS: 15-25%
- Mid-market SaaS: 10-15%
- Enterprise SaaS: 5-10%
- Best in class: < 5%

### Gross Margin
- SaaS (subscription): 70-85%
- Marketplace: 20-40% (take rate)
- Vertical SaaS with services: 50-70%

### CAC Payback
- SMB: 6-12 months
- Mid-market: 12-18 months
- Enterprise: 18-24 months
- Best in class: < 12 months across segments

### LTV:CAC
- Healthy: 3:1
- Great: 5:1
- Best in class: 7:1+

### Burn Multiple
- Acceptable: < 2x
- Healthy: 1-1.5x
- Great: < 1x
- Best in class: < 0.5x (rule of 40+)

### Rule of 40
Growth rate (%) + EBITDA margin (%) ≥ 40
- Below 40: efficiency or growth problem
- 40-60: healthy
- 60+: best in class

### Magic Number
Net new ARR / S&M spend
- < 0.5: Slow down spending
- 0.5-1: OK but inefficient
- 1-1.5: Healthy
- > 1.5: Press accelerator

## ICP-Specific Benchmarks

### B2B SaaS — SMB (10-50 employees)
- Sales cycle: 1-4 weeks
- Average deal size: $1K-10K ACV
- Sales motion: Self-serve or inside sales
- Activation: Within 7 days
- NRR target: 105%+

### B2B SaaS — Mid-market (50-1000 employees)
- Sales cycle: 1-3 months
- Average deal size: $10K-100K ACV
- Sales motion: Inside sales + customer success
- Activation: Within 30 days
- NRR target: 110%+

### B2B SaaS — Enterprise (1000+ employees)
- Sales cycle: 3-12 months
- Average deal size: $100K-1M+ ACV
- Sales motion: Field sales + customer success
- Activation: Within 60-90 days
- NRR target: 120%+

### Consumer SaaS / DTC
- Conversion rate (visitor → trial): 1-5%
- Trial → paid: 10-30%
- Annual churn: 30-60%
- ARPU: $50-500/year
- LTV: $50-500
- CAC: $20-200

## Hiring Benchmarks (Engineering)

### Engineer-to-Revenue Ratio
- Pre-seed: $0-100K ARR per engineer
- Seed: $100K-300K ARR per engineer
- Series A: $300K-500K ARR per engineer
- Series B+: $500K-1M+ ARR per engineer

### Engineering as % of headcount
- Pre-product: 50-70%
- Post-product, pre-revenue: 50-60%
- Revenue-generating: 30-50%
