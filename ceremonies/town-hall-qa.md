# Ceremony: CEO Town Hall / AMA

## Purpose
Open Q&A session between CEO and the entire company. Builds transparency, surfaces concerns, and demonstrates leadership accountability.

## Frequency
Monthly (can be combined with all-hands or standalone).

## Format (45 min)

### Setup
- Anonymous question submission opens 48 hrs before
- Questions visible to all employees (they can upvote)
- CEO answers the top-voted questions in the session
- Live questions accepted after pre-submitted ones are addressed

### Rules
- No question is off-limits (within reason)
- CEO answers honestly. "I don't know" and "I can't share that yet" are valid.
- No deflecting to someone else (CEO takes accountability)
- Follow up on any "I'll get back to you" within 1 week

### Topics that usually come up
- Company performance and trajectory
- Hiring plans and team growth
- Compensation and equity
- Remote work and office policies
- Product strategy and roadmap
- Layoff rumors or restructuring
- Culture and values in practice

## Facilitation Tips
- Read the questions beforehand. Prepare thoughtful answers.
- Answer the hard questions first. It builds trust.
- Be vulnerable: "This has been a tough quarter. Here's what I'm doing about it."
- Keep answers concise. Long answers feel like filibustering.
- If the same question keeps coming up every month, the underlying issue isn't being addressed.

## Anti-Patterns
- Filtering out hard questions (destroys the purpose)
- CEO gives 10-minute answers (audience tunes out)
- Session happens but nothing changes (performative transparency)
- Only positive questions get airtime (employees notice)
