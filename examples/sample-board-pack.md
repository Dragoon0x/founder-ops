## Decisions Needed This Meeting

### Decision 1: Approve Enterprise Pivot
**The question:** Hire 4 enterprise-focused roles in Q2-Q3 (2 AEs, 1 CSM, 1 SE)
**The cost:** $720K in incremental burn over 9 months
**The bet:** Hit $300K enterprise ARR by Q4, $1M by mid-2027
**Risk if we don't:** Lose first-mover advantage, slower path to Series A
**Risk if we do:** 2 months less runway if we don't hit numbers

**My recommendation:** APPROVE
**Confidence:** 7/10
**What would change my mind:** If Q2 enterprise pipeline doesn't grow 3x, pause hiring 2 of the 4 roles.

### Decision 2: Approve Option Pool Refresh
**The question:** Top off option pool by 4% (currently 9% available, target 13%)
**Why:** New hires for enterprise + we'll need exec hire by Q4
**Dilution:** Founders/existing investors take 4% dilution
**Alternative:** Wait for Series A and refresh as part of round (may be too late)

**My recommendation:** APPROVE
**Confidence:** 8/10

### Decision 3: Approve Series A Timing
**The question:** Start Series A in Q4 (Oct 2026) vs delay to Q1 2027
**Trade-off:**
- Q4: Less data (less ARR), but warmer market typically
- Q1: More data, but seasonality risk + runway concern

**My recommendation:** Start preparation now, decide on launch by August
**Confidence:** 6/10
**Need from board:** Intros to potential lead investors over next 60 days
