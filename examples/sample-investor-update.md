**Subject: [Company] — March 2026 Update**

## Highlights
- Closed 3 enterprise deals worth $180K ARR (first 5-figure deals)
- Hired [Name] as Head of Engineering from [prev company]
- Product hit 40% weekly active ratio for the first time

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Feb 2026 | Mar 2026 | Trend |
|--------|---------|---------|-------|
| MRR | $34K | $49K | ↑ 44% |
| New customers | 12 | 19 | ↑ 58% |

## What's Working

- **Enterprise channel**: Outbound to VP Eng audience converting at 12% (up from 6%). Three closed deals this month prove we can sell to orgs with 100+ eng.
- **Product velocity**: Shipped 14 features this month, including the top 3 requested in last quarter's user survey.
- **Retention**: 114% NRR shows customers are expanding usage, not just sticking around.

## What's Not Working

- **Inbound SEO**: Content hasn't moved the needle. 3 months of investment, minimal organic signups. Evaluating whether to continue or redirect budget.
- **Hiring PM**: 47 candidates interviewed, zero offers extended. Our bar is too high for current comp. Considering raising compensation ceiling or rescoping the role.
- **Activation rate**: Dropped from 67% to 62% after the onboarding redesign. Investigating whether it's the new flow or cohort mix.

## Asks

Specific help would be appreciated:

1. **Intros to Head of Engineering at Series B SaaS** (especially horizontal SaaS). We're landing with VPs but want to expand to CTO level.
2. **Feedback on our pricing page** — considering moving from seat-based to usage-based. Anyone's been through this?
3. **Candidates for Head of Product** — senior PM with B2B SaaS experience, ideally someone who's gone 0-to-1 before.

## Looking Ahead — April Priorities

1. Launch self-serve tier (reduce sales cycle for smaller customers)
2. Hit $75K MRR (requires 50% of current sales cycle to close)
3. Start early conversations for Series A (not raising yet, testing market)

## Thank you

Your intros to [Name] and [Name] this month were instrumental in the enterprise wins. Couldn't do this without you.

— [Founder name]
