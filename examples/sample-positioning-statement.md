## Stage 1: Competitive alternatives

**What would our ideal customers do if we did not exist?**

Not: "use another CRM" — that is how we would compete, not how they would solve.

The real alternatives are:
1. **Enter CRM data manually after hours.** Reps spend 45-90 minutes each evening logging calls. Data quality is low; 30% of calls are never logged.
2. **Use voice memos + manual entry later.** Rep records a voice note; an admin or the rep later types it in. Two steps, brittle, slow.
3. **Rely on memory + weekly pipeline review.** Manager extracts data from reps in weekly 1:1s. Half the nuance is already lost.
4. **Add a general-purpose voice-to-text tool (Otter, etc).** Gets transcript; does not structure CRM entries. Still leaves manual sync work.

Our positioning fights against the evening-data-entry workflow, not against Salesforce.

---

## Stage 2: Unique attributes

**What capabilities do we have that the alternatives do not?**

Raw attribute list:
- Offline-capable mobile app (works without cell signal; syncs later)
- Voice-first capture (30-second capture post-call, structured output)
- Field-sales-tuned language model (91% entity accuracy on specialized vocabulary)
- Direct CRM sync (17 CRMs supported; Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.)
- Customer-specific model tuning (Helio Learn)
- <2 second latency for call-to-CRM workflow
- Works with any existing CRM (integration, not replacement)

---

## Stage 3: Value

**So what? What value do these attributes deliver?**

- **Time reclaimed.** Evidence: Mountain Valley Co-op reclaimed 6 hours/rep/week.
- **CRM data quality.** Evidence: data completeness went from 47% to 89% in pilot deployments.
- **Better pipeline visibility.** Managers see accurate pipeline because reps actually log calls.
- **Rep quality-of-life.** Evidence: 8 of 10 surveyed reps said Helio "makes Sunday night better."
- **Better forecasting.** Evidence: 2 customers reported forecast accuracy improved 15-20pp.

---

## Stage 4: Customer

**Who specifically values this?**

Tightest definition of ICP (ideal customer profile):

- **Industry:** Sales-led businesses with field reps covering geographic territory
- **Size:** Mid-market, 50-500 employees; 10-100 field reps
- **Structure:** Reps drive between appointments; desk time is evening/office
- **Tech:** Has a CRM (does not matter which; integration-agnostic)
- **Pain:** Evening CRM entry or Monday-morning pipeline ambiguity is a named problem
- **Budget:** $15k-150k ACV range; can buy without board approval

Weak fit (we should disqualify):
- Inside-sales-only teams (our moat is gone)
- Pre-CRM teams (they have other problems first)
- Over-1000 field reps (enterprise motion; our GTM is not ready)
- Highly regulated sectors requiring specific deployments we do not offer

---

## Stage 5: Market category

**What is the frame that makes our value obvious?**

Category candidates we considered:
- "Field sales CRM" — collides with legacy CRMs; confusing
- "Voice-first sales tool" — feature-framed; we are more than voice
- "Revenue intelligence for field sales" — category exists; dominated by Gong et al
- "AI sales assistant" — generic; crowded
- "Field sales command center" — puts us in a category of our own shaping

Chosen: **Field sales command center** (with "AI-native" implicit)

Framing: "You already have a CRM. Helio is the command center that makes your CRM work for reps who sell from the road."

## Final positioning statement (internal)

> **For mid-market sales organizations with 10+ field reps**, Helio is a **field sales command center** that **captures calls by voice and turns them into structured CRM data automatically**. Unlike **classic CRMs that require manual entry or generic voice tools that do not understand sales**, Helio works **offline, in under 2 seconds per call, with 91% accuracy on sales-specific data**. Customers like Mountain Valley Co-op have **reclaimed 6 hours per rep per week and improved pipeline data completeness from 47% to 89%**.

## What we explicitly do NOT claim

- Replace your CRM (we integrate, not replace)
- Work for inside sales (our edge doesn't apply)
- Enterprise-grade deployment at 1000+ reps (not yet)
- Solve forecasting or coaching (adjacent problems, future products)
