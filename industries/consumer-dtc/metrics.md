# Consumer / DTC — Key Metrics & Benchmarks

## Core DTC Metrics

### 1. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Blended CAC**: Total marketing spend / new customers
- **Channel CAC**: By channel for optimization
- **Benchmarks (physical goods, $100 AOV)**:
  - <$30: Excellent
  - $30-60: Good
  - $60-100: Concerning (need high LTV)
  - $100+: Broken unless LTV is very high

### 2. Average Order Value (AOV)
- **What**: Average $ value per order
- **Levers**: Bundles, upsells, free shipping thresholds, premium tiers
- **Benchmarks vary wildly** by category

### 3. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- **Formula**: AOV × Order Frequency × Avg Customer Lifespan × Gross Margin
- **Realistic time horizon**: 12-24 months for most DTC
- **Key**: Calculate with REAL gross margin (after returns, shipping, support)

### 4. LTV:CAC Ratio
- **Healthy**: 3:1 or higher
- **Excellent**: 5:1+
- **Broken**: <2:1

### 5. Payback Period
- **What**: Months until LTV pays back CAC
- **Benchmarks**:
  - <6 months: Very healthy
  - 6-12 months: Healthy
  - 12-18 months: Acceptable for high-LTV
  - 18+ months: Needs venture capital to fund growth

### 6. Repeat Purchase Rate
- **% of customers who buy again within 90 days**
- **By category**:
  - Skincare/cosmetics: 30-40%
  - Apparel: 20-30%
  - Mattresses/furniture: <5% (low frequency)
  - Subscription: 80%+ (or churn problem)

### 7. Gross Margin
- **Physical goods**:
  - <30%: Very tough
  - 30-50%: Below average
  - 50-65%: Average
  - 65-75%: Good
  - 75%+: Excellent
- **Important**: Calculate WITH shipping, returns, packaging

### 8. Contribution Margin
- **Formula**: AOV - COGS - Shipping - Returns - Payment Fees
- **What's left to spend on CAC and overhead**
- **If contribution margin < CAC**: Losing money on every order

## Channel Performance Tracking

| Channel | Track |
|---------|-------|
| Paid social | CAC, ROAS, frequency cap |
| Search ads | CAC, conversion rate, branded vs non-branded |
| SEO | Organic traffic, conversion rate, CAC of $0 |
| Influencer | CPM, engagement, attributable conversions |
| Email | Open rate, CTR, revenue per send |
| SMS | CTR, conversion rate, opt-in rate |
| Affiliates | Commission, fraud rate, contribution to LTV |
| Referrals | Referral rate, LTV of referred vs paid |

## Cohort Analysis (Critical for DTC)

Track monthly cohorts:
- Month 0: Initial purchase
- Month 1, 2, 3...: Repeat purchases
- Calculate cumulative LTV per cohort

Healthy patterns:
- Cohort LTV continues growing past 6 months
- Newer cohorts perform similar to or better than older
- 30%+ of cohort makes 2nd purchase within 90 days

Warning patterns:
- Cohort LTV plateaus at 1 purchase
- Newer cohorts performing worse (channel quality degrading)
- High Month 0 then crickets

## Stage-Based Benchmarks

| Stage | Annual Revenue | Customers | Marketing Spend | Team |
|-------|---------------|-----------|----------------|------|
| Pre-launch | $0 | <100 | $0-10K (mostly content) | 1-2 |
| Early | $0-500K | 100-2K | 10-30% of revenue | 2-5 |
| Growth | $500K-5M | 2K-25K | 25-40% of revenue | 5-20 |
| Scale | $5M-50M | 25K-500K | 20-35% of revenue | 20-100 |
| Mature | $50M+ | 500K+ | 15-25% of revenue | 100+ |

## Inventory Metrics (Physical Goods)

- **Days of Inventory**: Inventory / (COGS / 365). Target: 30-90 days
- **Stockout rate**: % of orders that can't be fulfilled. Target: <2%
- **Sell-through**: Units sold / Units bought. Target: 80%+ within season
- **Returns rate**: By category, but watch trends

## Brand Health Metrics

- **Brand search volume**: Organic searches for your brand name (track via Google Search Console)
- **Direct traffic %**: Higher = stronger brand
- **Net Promoter Score**: 30+ good, 50+ excellent, 70+ exceptional
- **Repeat customer %**: % of revenue from existing vs new
- **Earned media mentions**: Press, blogs, social mentions
