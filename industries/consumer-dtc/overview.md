# Consumer / DTC — Industry Overview

## What "Wins" Looks Like

Direct-to-consumer brands sell physical or digital products straight to customers
(no retail middleman). The category has matured from "easy growth via Facebook ads"
to "build a real brand and supply chain."

Win conditions:
- Repeat purchase rate (LTV > acquisition cost)
- Brand affinity (organic mention, referrals)
- Healthy margins (60%+ gross margin for digital, 40%+ for physical)
- Supply chain control
- Community / loyalty

## Categories

### Physical Goods (DTC)
- **Examples**: Allbirds, Warby Parker, Glossier, Casper, Away
- **Margins**: 40-65% gross
- **Repeat rate**: Varies wildly by category (mattresses ≠ skincare)
- **Cost**: COGS, shipping, returns, customer support

### Subscription Boxes
- **Examples**: Dollar Shave Club, Stitch Fix, Birchbox
- **Margins**: 30-50%
- **Churn challenge**: Subscription fatigue, decision fatigue
- **Strength**: Predictable revenue, customer data

### Digital Products / Content
- **Examples**: MasterClass, Calm, Headspace, Duolingo
- **Margins**: 70-90% gross
- **Challenge**: User acquisition cost rising
- **Strength**: No inventory, no shipping

### Consumer SaaS
- **Examples**: Notion (consumer use), Spotify, Netflix
- **Margins**: 60-85%
- **Pricing**: Lower than B2B, but volume
- **Challenge**: High churn relative to B2B

### Consumer Fintech
- **Examples**: Robinhood, Cash App, Chime
- **Different unit economics**: Often interchange-based, lending margins, premium tiers

## The Acquisition Reality

Consumer acquisition has gotten dramatically harder since ~2018:

| Channel | 2015 CAC | 2025 CAC | Status |
|---------|----------|----------|--------|
| Facebook ads | $5-15 | $40-150 | Saturated, expensive |
| Instagram ads | $3-10 | $30-100 | Saturated |
| Google search | $5-25 | $25-100 | Competitive |
| Influencer | $10-50 | $30-200 | Variable |
| TikTok | N/A | $5-40 | Growing |
| Affiliates | $3-15 | $15-50 | Niche-dependent |
| Word of mouth | $0 | $0 | Always best |

**Implication**: You can't rely on paid ads alone. Must build:
- Strong organic content / SEO
- Community / brand loyalty
- Partnerships
- Direct customer relationships (email, SMS)

## Ideal Founder Profile

- Brand intuition (taste, design)
- Operational rigor (supply chain doesn't forgive)
- Customer obsession (consumer expectations are unforgiving)
- Patience (brand-building is slow)

## Founder Mistakes (Common)

1. **Relying on paid acquisition only** — Channel saturates, CAC explodes
2. **Skipping brand work** — Commoditized in 12 months
3. **Underpricing** — Consumer brands need margin to absorb returns/shipping/CAC
4. **No customer service investment** — Consumer reviews kill you fast
5. **Inventory mistakes** — Tied-up cash, dead stock, or stockouts
6. **Ignoring email/SMS** — Owned channels matter more as paid gets harder
7. **Premature international expansion** — Different shipping, customs, returns, tax
