# Healthtech — Metrics

## By sub-vertical

### Provider SaaS / EHR adjacent
- **ARR per provider (seat).** $2k-20k/year depending on specialty
- **Time to EHR integration.** Weeks/months; under 6 weeks is elite
- **NPS from clinicians.** Clinicians complain; 30+ is strong
- **Workflow tasks completed in your tool per clinician per day.** Usage signal
- **Documentation time saved per clinician per day.** Outcome metric

### Telehealth / virtual care
- **Visits per member per month (PMPM).** For employer-sold programs
- **No-show rate.** Target under 10%
- **Clinical quality measures.** HEDIS, NCQA, specialty guidelines compliance
- **Member acquisition cost.** Employer CAC divided by per-employee count
- **Visit completion to outcome rate.** % of visits leading to intended clinical action

### Digital therapeutics (DTx)
- **Activation rate.** % of prescribed users who complete onboarding. Typically 40-60%
- **Adherence.** % of users completing prescribed protocol. 30-55% realistic
- **Clinical outcome vs. control.** Effect size vs. standard of care
- **Reimbursement per patient.** $/enrolled member
- **Net promoter score.** 40+ for health apps is strong

### At-risk / capitated care
- **MLR (Medical Loss Ratio).** Claims paid / premium received. Below 85% is healthy for the risk-bearing entity
- **Risk-adjusted cost per member per month.** Against benchmark
- **ED utilization rate.** ER visits per 1000 members per year
- **Star ratings / HEDIS measures.** For Medicare Advantage adjacent models
- **Readmission rate.** 30-day, 90-day

### Payer SaaS
- **ARR per covered life managed.** $0.10-$2.00 PMPM
- **Lives under management.** Scale metric
- **Administrative savings delivered.** $/claim or $/call

### Medical devices
- **Units shipped.** Per quarter
- **ASP (Average Selling Price).** And trend
- **Revenue per installed base per year.** Services, consumables
- **Utilization rate.** Devices used / devices installed

## Cross-cutting metrics

- **Sales cycle length.** Critical for capital planning
- **Pilot-to-contract conversion.** Pilots that become paying contracts
- **Integration time.** EHR / data / admin integration time
- **Compliance incident rate.** HIPAA incidents, breach rate
- **Regulatory milestones.** For device/DTx companies: submission, clearance, labeling

## Healthtech board deck metrics

1. ARR (or PMPM revenue)
2. Pipeline by stage with $ weighted
3. Pilot count and conversion trend
4. Clinical outcomes (if applicable)
5. Regulatory status (RAG)
6. Compliance posture (SOC 2, HITRUST, HIPAA incidents YTD)
7. Sales cycle length by segment
8. Implementation / integration backlog
9. Cash and runway
10. Top 3 risks (usually: regulatory, reimbursement, sales cycle elongation)
