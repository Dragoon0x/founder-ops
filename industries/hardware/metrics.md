# Hardware — Metrics

## Product economics

- **BOM cost.** Bill of materials, component cost
- **Landed COGS.** BOM + assembly + logistics + duties + yield loss
- **Gross margin.** (ASP - COGS) / ASP. Target 40%+ at launch, 50%+ mature
- **ASP (Average Selling Price).** Weighted across channels
- **Channel margin stack.** Retailer margin + distributor margin + company margin
- **Yield rate.** % of manufactured units that pass QC. Target 95%+ at ramp
- **DPPM (Defective Parts Per Million).** Field quality; under 1000 DPPM is strong
- **RMA rate.** Return merchandise authorization, % of units returned

## Operations

- **Inventory turns.** COGS / average inventory, annualized
- **DIO (Days Inventory Outstanding).** 365 / turns
- **Cycle time.** From PO to delivered finished goods
- **Fill rate.** % of orders shipped complete on time. 95%+ healthy
- **On-time delivery.** From CM to warehouse
- **Scrap rate.** % of material scrapped in production
- **Warranty reserve.** % of revenue reserved for warranty claims

## Commercial

- **Sell-in vs. sell-through.** Units shipped to channel vs. to end users
- **Weeks of supply in channel.** Channel inventory / sell-through rate
- **Attach rate (SaaS).** If razor/blade, % of hardware buyers subscribing to software
- **LTV with services.** Hardware revenue + services revenue - service costs
- **CAC by channel.** DTC, retail, enterprise distinct

## Supply chain

- **Component lead times.** By part. Critical parts on a lead-time dashboard
- **Supplier concentration.** % of BOM from top 1, top 5 suppliers
- **Dual-source coverage %.** % of BOM with qualified second source
- **Tariff exposure.** $ / unit by country of origin
- **Inventory aging.** % over 90/180/365 days

## Quality and service

- **Field return rate.** Returns / units in field, by vintage
- **Field failure rate (annualized).** Field failures / installed base
- **MTBF (Mean Time Between Failures).** Where applicable
- **Warranty cost per unit.** Actual warranty spend / units sold
- **Support ticket rate.** Tickets / installed base
- **First-time fix rate.** For serviceable products

## Hardware board deck metrics

1. Revenue (by SKU, by channel)
2. Gross margin (ASP, COGS trend)
3. Units shipped; units in channel; sell-through rate
4. Inventory ($), DIO, aging
5. Yield trend, DPPM trend
6. Field return rate, warranty cost / unit
7. PO-to-delivery cycle time
8. Supplier risk (concentration, dual-source, tariff)
9. Cash, runway, working-capital position
10. Product/engineering milestones (next product DVT, PVT)
