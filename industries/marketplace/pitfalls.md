# Marketplace — Common Founder Pitfalls

## Cold Start Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Why it kills you | Fix |
|---------|------------------|-----|
| Launching both sides simultaneously | Neither has enough density | Single-side seed, then layer on |
| National launch | Liquidity diluted across geos | Hyperlocal: pick 1 city, win it |
| Trying to monetize at zero scale | Suppliers leave for free alternatives | Subsidize early; monetize at liquidity |
| Faking supply with low quality | One bad experience = trust loss | Hand-curate quality, even if slower |
| Ignoring frequency | Low frequency = no compounding | Pick high-frequency category if possible |

## Trust & Safety Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Why it kills you | Fix |
|---------|------------------|-----|
| No verification | Bad actors, fraud, safety incidents | Identity verification (varies by category) |
| No reviews/ratings | New users have no signal | Build review system from day 1 |
| No dispute resolution | Bad transactions kill trust | Clear policy + actual humans for edge cases |
| No moderation team | Spam/abuse takes over | Outsource initially, build later |
| No insurance/escrow | High-value transactions don't happen | For high-value: escrow; for risk: insurance |

## Off-Platform Leakage Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Why it kills you | Fix |
|---------|------------------|-----|
| Buyer + seller exchange contact info | They transact off-platform | Withhold contact until first transaction completes |
| No payment integration | They invoice each other | Mandatory in-platform payments |
| No review system | No incentive to stay | Reviews tied to platform identity |
| Easy direct booking | They book repeat directly | Loyalty/discount for in-platform repeats |
| No platform value-add | Just a directory | Add value: insurance, payments, scheduling, support |

## Pricing & Take Rate Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Why it kills you | Fix |
|---------|------------------|-----|
| Take rate too high too early | Suppliers leave | Start low; raise as you prove value |
| Same take rate across categories | Some categories can bear more | Vary by category, by tier |
| No payment fee transparency | Suppliers don't know real take | Show breakdown; be honest |
| Surprise fees | Trust erosion | All fees disclosed before transaction |
| No volume discounts for power supply | Power suppliers leave for competitors | Tiered take rate (lower for high volume) |

## Network Effect Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Why it kills you | Fix |
|---------|------------------|-----|
| Assuming network effects exist | Most marketplaces don't have them | Test: would a new entrant struggle to compete? |
| Only direct network effects | Often weak | Build data network effects (better matching) |
| No defensibility against giants | Amazon/Meta enter your category | Vertical specialization + community |
| Ignoring power users | They drive most GMV | Top 5% of users = 50%+ of GMV |

## Operational Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Why it kills you | Fix |
|---------|------------------|-----|
| Customer support not scaling | One viral incident, no team | Tiered support; AI for tier 1 |
| Manual matching | Doesn't scale past 1Ks | Algorithmic matching by Series A |
| No fraud prevention | Lose 5-10% to fraud | Automated fraud scoring + manual review |
| No multi-currency / language | Can't expand internationally | Build for it from year 1 if global |
| No mobile experience | Most marketplace users are mobile | Native apps by Series A |

## Geographic Expansion Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Why it kills you | Fix |
|---------|------------------|-----|
| Expanding before proving liquidity in one geo | Spreads team thin | Win city #1 completely first |
| Same playbook everywhere | Local dynamics vary | Local launches, local champions |
| Ignoring regulation | Uber-style regulatory wars | Engage locally before launch |
| No local partnerships | Can't scale supply fast enough | Partner with local incumbents |

## Investor / Fundraising Pitfalls

| Pitfall | Why it kills you | Fix |
|---------|------------------|-----|
| GMV-only metrics | Investors discount unprofitable GMV | Show take rate progression and unit economics |
| Hiding cohort decay | Won't survive due diligence | Be honest, show plan to fix |
| No path to profitability | Marketplaces eat capital | Show clear path, even if 3+ years out |
| Vague "network effects" | Buzzword without proof | Show direct evidence: cost to acquire is dropping |
