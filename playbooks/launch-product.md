# Playbook: Launch Product

## Two Kinds of Launch

Most founders conflate two different things:

**Soft launch:** "It's out. Here's the URL. Go use it."
Goal: Get 100 first users, learn what breaks.
Audience: Your existing community + network.
Duration: 2-4 weeks.

**Big launch:** "We're ready for the world."
Goal: 10K-100K first impressions, 1-5K new users.
Audience: Press, Twitter/LinkedIn, Product Hunt, HN.
Duration: 1 coordinated day with 2-week tail.

Do the soft launch FIRST. Always.

## Soft Launch Playbook (2-4 weeks)

### Week 1: Prep
- [ ] Core flow works for 90% of users on your main browsers/devices
- [ ] Landing page that explains the product in 10 seconds
- [ ] Analytics tracking (user, key events, errors)
- [ ] Support channel (email or Intercom) you personally monitor
- [ ] Onboarding: either zero-friction OR a personal Loom from you

### Week 2: Ship to 20 warm users
- DM 20 people in your network who'd care
- Watch what they do (session recordings, analytics)
- Fix the top 3 breakages before adding more users

### Week 3-4: Expand to 100
- Share in 2-3 communities where you're already a known member
- DON'T post on HN or Product Hunt yet
- Goal: 10-20 active users, 5 people using weekly

### Soft launch success = 5+ unprompted "this is great, when can I [X]?"

If you don't get that, the product isn't ready. Don't big-launch broken product.

## Big Launch Playbook (2-week sprint)

### -14 days: Assets
- [ ] Demo video (90 seconds, no music, show the product)
- [ ] Landing page with: hero, 3 features, social proof, CTA
- [ ] Pricing page (yes, even if free — set expectations)
- [ ] Docs (the top 5 things people will need)
- [ ] "Who we are" narrative (founder story, 200 words)

### -7 days: Amplification network
- [ ] Line up 10-20 people who will share on launch day (investors, advisors, happy customers)
- [ ] Ask 3 for specific testimonials you can use
- [ ] Set up Product Hunt ship (asset uploader)
- [ ] Draft HN post (you'll post FROM founder account; PH can be a team acct)
- [ ] Write launch tweet thread (12-15 tweets)

### -3 days: Press (if you want any)
- [ ] Email 10 relevant journalists with a short personal note + under-embargo link
- [ ] Offer them exclusive angle (first demo, first interview)
- [ ] Expect 1-3 replies, 0-1 publication on launch day

### Launch day (choose Tuesday or Wednesday, 6:00 AM PT for HN/PH)

**Hour 0 (6 AM):** Post on HN, Product Hunt, Twitter. Exactly one thing each.

**Hour 1-3:** Reply to every comment. Fast. Thoughtful. No corp-speak.

**Hour 4-6:** Newsletter to your subscribers. Email network. Watch metrics.

**Hour 7+:** Share to relevant Slack/Discord communities. NOT spam — with
personal context for each.

**End of day:** Internal retro. What worked, what didn't. What's broken?

### +1 day: Follow-up
- Thank-you notes to everyone who helped amplify
- Fix top 3 issues from launch day traffic
- Personal outreach to top 20 signups who look like ICP

### +7 days: Second wave
- Post lessons learned / stats tweet ("We launched last week — here's what happened")
- Case study from launch-day customers
- Ongoing SEO / content cadence

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Soft launch target | Big launch target |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Signups | 50-100 | 1K-10K |
| Activation rate (first real action) | 40%+ | 25%+ (lower because broader audience) |
| D1 retention | 30%+ | 20%+ |
| D7 retention | 15%+ | 10%+ |
| HN/PH rank | N/A | Top 5 on launch day |
| Press | 0 | 1-3 small publications, maybe 1 tier-1 |
| Paying customers | 3-10 | 20-100 |

## Post-Launch Gotchas

**Week 2-3: Traffic drops 80%.** Normal. The signal is NOT launch day.
It's week 4-5 organic traffic + retention.

**Week 4 check:** Are any of the launch-day signups active? If not, launch
didn't deliver real users, just impressions.

**Real signal:** WoW organic signups 30 days after launch are 2-3x your
pre-launch baseline.

## Anti-Patterns

- Launching without any launch-day users already warm (dead on arrival)
- Launching broken product to look momentum-y
- Over-spending on launch (PR firm, video production) — ROI is low
- "Launching" repeatedly — diminishing returns after 2x
- Launching with no clear ICP — broad audience = nobody audience
