# Playbook: Series A Readiness

## When to Use This
You raised a seed 12-18 months ago. You need to decide: start Series A now,
wait 2 more quarters, or pivot and extend runway.

## The Real Bar (B2B SaaS, 2026)

| Metric | Series A Bar | Why |
|--------|--------------|-----|
| ARR | $1-3M | Repeatable revenue, not a science project |
| Net new ARR (last 3 months) | $100-300K/month | Shows acceleration, not stagnation |
| Gross retention | >90% | Product delivers, customers stay |
| Net retention | >110% | Expansion works, upside exists |
| Gross margin | >70% | Software economics, not services |
| CAC payback | <18 months | Unit economics work |
| Team | 8-20 people | Enough to prove a motion, not overstaffed |
| Burn multiple | <2x | $1 burn → $0.50+ new ARR |
| Customer count | 30+ paying | De-risked from 1 big logo |

## Consumer Bar

| Metric | Series A Bar |
|--------|--------------|
| MAU | 100K-1M+ |
| D30 retention | 25%+ |
| DAU/MAU | 20%+ |
| Revenue (if monetizing) | $500K-2M ARR |

## Marketplace Bar

| Metric | Series A Bar |
|--------|--------------|
| GMV run rate | $10-50M |
| Take rate | 10%+ net |
| Liquidity | >30% search-to-fill |
| Cohort month-12 retention | >60% |

## Diagnostic Checklist

### Do you have the story?
- [ ] Can you explain your category in 1 sentence (not a list of features)?
- [ ] Can you name the top 3 competitors and how you beat each?
- [ ] Can you show a growth curve that accelerates, not just grows?
- [ ] Can you name 3 "lighthouse" customers by name?

### Do you have the team?
- [ ] Do you have a head of something (Eng, Product, GTM) besides you?
- [ ] Are you below the Dunbar number but have a clear path to 30+ people?
- [ ] Is there a critical gap (VP Sales, CTO) you know you need to hire?

### Do you have the model?
- [ ] Has the GTM motion been run by 2+ people, not just the founder?
- [ ] Are new customers closing at predictable conversion rates?
- [ ] Can you name the 3 channels that actually work?

## Decision Matrix

| State | Recommendation |
|-------|----------------|
| You hit metrics + momentum accelerating | Raise now. Window is best now. |
| You hit metrics, growth flat | Spend 2 quarters pushing growth, then raise. Flat growth = harder story. |
| You miss metrics by 30%+, strong unit economics | Bridge or small extension. Series A will be hard. |
| You miss metrics badly, weak unit economics | Do NOT raise Series A. Fix unit economics or pivot. |

## Red Flags That Prevent Series A

- **Customer concentration >25% of ARR from one customer**
- **Founder is the only seller** (VCs need to see the motion works without you)
- **You cannot articulate why customers pick you over incumbents**
- **Margin below 60%** (services business in SaaS clothing)
- **High churn despite high growth** (leaky bucket — growth will stall)
