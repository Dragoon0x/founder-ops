# Disclaimer

## Educational & Experimental Purpose

Founder-Ops is provided strictly for **educational and experimental purposes only.**
It is a set of workflows and templates designed to help founders think through
common startup problems. It is NOT a substitute for professional advice.

## NOT Legal Advice

The legal mode (`/founder-ops legal`) flags common issues in contracts and
vendor agreements. It is NOT legal advice. Always consult a qualified attorney
before signing any contract, especially ones involving:
- IP transfer or assignment
- Equity or stock
- Employment
- Partnerships or joint ventures
- Fundraising
- Disputes or litigation

## NOT Financial Advice

The finance mode (`/founder-ops finance`) helps you model scenarios. It is NOT
financial, investment, or tax advice. Consult a CPA, CFO, or financial advisor
for decisions affecting your company's finances.

## NOT Investment Advice

The fundraise mode (`/founder-ops fundraise`) helps you research investors
and draft outreach. It does NOT evaluate whether to raise, from whom, or on
what terms. Those decisions require professional advice.

## DYOR (Do Your Own Research)

- Every output is a draft, not a final answer
- Every recommendation requires your judgment
- Every template needs customization to your specific situation
- No output should be used verbatim without review
- The AI does not know your full context — it only knows what you've told it

## Output Quality

AI outputs can be:
- Factually wrong
- Subtly misleading
- Missing important context
- Biased by training data
- Inconsistent across runs

Always verify before acting. Especially for:
- Legal language
- Financial projections
- Investor communications
- Hiring decisions
- Contract terms
- Public statements

## Human-in-the-Loop

Founder-ops NEVER takes autonomous action on your behalf. It does not:
- Send emails
- Sign contracts
- Publish content
- Schedule meetings
- Make investment decisions
- Commit to hires

Every output is a draft for YOU to review, edit, and decide on.

## Privacy & Data

Your config files (founder.yml, company.yml, product.yml, investors.yml) contain
sensitive information. Keep them:
- Gitignored in your personal fork
- Not shared in public channels
- Backed up securely
- Deleted if no longer needed
