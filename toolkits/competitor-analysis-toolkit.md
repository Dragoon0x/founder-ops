# Competitor Analysis Toolkit

## Competitive Landscape Map

### Market positioning matrix
Plot competitors on two axes relevant to your market:
- X-axis: often Simplicity ↔ Power (or SMB ↔ Enterprise)
- Y-axis: often Price ↔ Value (or Niche ↔ Platform)

Identify the gap where no competitor sits strongly. That's your opportunity.

### Win/Loss Analysis Process
For every deal (won or lost):
1. Record the outcome and primary competitor (if any)
2. Ask the buyer: "What was the deciding factor?"
3. Categorize: price, product, relationship, timing, brand, other
4. Track win rate by competitor over time

Monthly review:
- Win rate by competitor (improving or declining?)
- Most common loss reasons (product gap? Pricing? Sales execution?)
- Deals lost to "no decision" (often more important than competitive losses)
