# Speed is a feature

> Especially early, how fast you ship and respond is a product differentiator that larger companies cannot match.

## The principle

- Respond to customer issues in minutes when possible.
- Ship fixes the same day the bug is reported — even small ones. The customer remembers.
- Make decisions fast. Most decisions are reversible. Treat them that way.
- The cost of a slow decision is often higher than the cost of a wrong decision.

## Signals it's time to act

- Speed doesn't mean recklessness. It means: default to fast; slow down only when the decision is expensive and irreversible.
- Two-way doors (easy to undo): decide in minutes. One-way doors (hard to undo): take the time.

## Notes

- A startup that moves 2x faster than a competitor doesn't just ship twice as much — they learn twice as fast. Compounds over quarters.
