# Culture is what you tolerate

> Your real culture is not on your values page. It's in what you accept and what you fire for.

## The principle

- If you say 'we value candor' but tolerate passive-aggression in meetings, your culture is passive-aggressive.
- If you say 'customer first' but your #1 rep hasn't sent a personal customer note in a year, your culture isn't customer-first.
- If you say 'move fast' but approvals take 3 weeks, your culture is slow.

## Signals it's time to act

- The fastest way to fix culture: fire for cultural violations earlier than you would for performance.
- The slowest way to fix culture: write a new values document.

## Notes

- What you celebrate publicly, and what you fire quietly, become your culture. Nothing else.
