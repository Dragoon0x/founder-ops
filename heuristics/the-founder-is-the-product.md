# The founder is the product

> Until ~$1M ARR, the founder IS the product, the sales deck, the brand, and the recruiter. Accept it and work with it, not against it.

## The principle

- You can't delegate what you haven't defined. Define your sales motion by doing it first.
- Don't hire a head of X before you've done X well enough to train someone.
- This phase ends around $1-3M ARR, usually right after your first great non-founder hire.

## Signals it's time to act

- The failure mode is a founder who tries to scale via delegation before they've scaled via doing.
- The other failure mode is a founder who can't ever let go.

## Notes

- When you finally hire someone who's better than you at a function, it will feel weird. That's the point.
