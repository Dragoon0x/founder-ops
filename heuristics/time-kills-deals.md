# Time kills deals

> Every week a deal sits, it's more likely to die.

## The principle

- Champions change jobs. Budgets move. Priorities shift. Competitors sneak in.
- Your job in sales is to create urgency legitimately — not manufacture it.
- Legitimate urgency: a pricing change, a product change, a renewal window, a quarter-end that affects THEM (not you).
- Illegitimate urgency: 'End of quarter discount' — every buyer has heard this 200 times.

## Signals it's time to act

- If a deal goes silent for 2 weeks: structured re-engagement email. If 4 weeks: direct 'is this dead?' message. If 6 weeks: it's dead, move on.
- Multi-threading (multiple stakeholders on the customer side) cuts deal risk in half. Do it early.

## Notes

- The best salespeople are the fastest at moving on from dead deals.
