# Metrics first, narrative second

> Founders who lead with narrative are either hiding or haven't looked yet.

## The principle

- Every leadership meeting starts with the scoreboard. No exceptions.
- If you can't produce a scoreboard for a function, that function doesn't exist yet. Build the scoreboard first.
- 'I feel like growth is slowing' is a starting point. 'MRR growth is 12% MoM for 3 months, down from 22%' is a place you can act from.

## Signals it's time to act

- The number you don't want to look at is the number you most need to look at.
- If your monthly update is 3 paragraphs of narrative and 2 charts, flip it.

## Notes

- Investors pattern-match harder to narrative than founders realize. Lead with numbers and you'll stand out.
