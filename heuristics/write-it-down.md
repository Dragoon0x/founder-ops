# Write it down

> Decisions that aren't written down didn't happen. Arguments you've had before will happen again.

## The principle

- Decision log: date, decision, who, why, what would make us revisit.
- Org chart: where it sits, who owns what, updated within 24h of any change.
- Pricing: every approved discount, every reason.
- Offers: every term, every exception, every reason for the exception.

## Signals it's time to act

- 30 minutes of writing saves 3 hours of re-litigating next quarter.
- New hires in month 4 will ask 'why do we do it this way' — the written record is your salvation.

## Notes

- If two smart people remember a meeting differently 2 weeks later, they're both right and both wrong. Write it down.
