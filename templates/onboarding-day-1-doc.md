## Week 2: First small contribution

Goal: Ship one small thing visible to customers OR meaningful to the team.

For engineering: Fix a small bug, add a small feature, improve a metric.
For design: Polish one screen, propose one improvement.
For GTM: Run one experiment, send one outreach campaign.
For ops: Document one process, automate one task.

The goal isn't impact — it's confidence. You shipped something on Week 2.

## Week 3: Take ownership

Pick one specific area you'll own. Examples:
- "I'll own the onboarding flow"
- "I'll own outbound to mid-market"
- "I'll own the data pipeline"

This becomes YOUR thing. You set goals, you make decisions, you report progress.
