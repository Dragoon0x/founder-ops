# Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1 — Cover
- Company name (large)
- One-line tagline (under 10 words)
- Your name + role
- Contact

## Slide 2 — Problem
- Specific pain, quantified
- Who has it (the customer, named if possible)
- Why it matters NOW

## Slide 3 — Solution
- One sentence of what you do
- Product demo screenshot (real, not mocked)
- Why this approach wins

## Slide 4 — Market
- TAM / SAM / SOM with real math
- Why the market is growing (macro trend)
- Top-down AND bottom-up validation

## Slide 5 — Product
- 2-3 real screenshots
- Key capability that matters to the customer
- What you don't do (scope clarity)

## Slide 6 — Traction
- Numbers up and to the right
- Revenue, customers, engagement — whichever is strongest
- Logo wall of key customers if applicable

## Slide 7 — Business Model
- How you charge (per seat / usage / flat)
- Unit economics (ARPU, gross margin, payback)
- NOT just "SaaS $49/mo" — show the math

## Slide 8 — Competition
- 2x2 matrix with meaningful axes
- Where you are, where competitors are
- Your honest differentiation

## Slide 9 — Team
- 3-5 people who matter
- Unfair advantage (why you specifically)
- Relevant past wins

## Slide 10 — Ask
- Specific amount ($X at $Y post)
- Use of funds (3-5 concrete categories)
- Timeline to next milestone
