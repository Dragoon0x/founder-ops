# Positioning Statement Template

## The Formula
For [target customer]
Who [situation or need]
[Product name] is [category]
That [key benefit / value proposition]
Unlike [primary competitive alternative]
We [key differentiator].

## Example
For B2B SaaS founders
Who need to make better decisions faster
Founder-ops is an AI-powered operating system
That provides structured frameworks, playbooks, and automation for every founder decision
Unlike generic business advice or one-size-fits-all consulting
We deliver stage-specific, opinionated guidance grounded in real benchmarks and operator experience.

## Testing Your Positioning
Ask 5 people in your target market to read it and answer:
1. Do you understand what this product does? (Clarity)
2. Do you believe this would help you? (Relevance)
3. How is this different from [competitor]? (Differentiation)
4. Would you try this? (Compelling)

If < 4/5 answer positively to any question, revise.

## Common Positioning Mistakes
1. Too broad: "For everyone who needs to be more productive" (who is this for, specifically?)
2. Too feature-focused: "We have AI-powered analytics with real-time dashboards" (so what?)
3. No competitive frame: Not mentioning what you replace leaves prospects confused
4. Jargon-heavy: "We leverage advanced ML to optimize cross-functional synergies" (please stop)
5. Aspirational instead of actual: Claiming differentiation you don't yet deliver
