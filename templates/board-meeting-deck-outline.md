# Board Meeting Deck — Outline

## 1. Cover
- Date, attendees, observers
- Quarter / topic theme

## 2. Agenda
- Time-boxed agenda
- "Decisions needed today" callout
- Pre-read materials referenced

## 3. TL;DR
- 3 things you want them to take away

## 4. Metrics Dashboard
- ARR / MRR (with growth rate vs last quarter)
- New / churned customers
- Net retention
- Burn / runway
- Team size
- Trend lines (last 6+ months)

## 5. Quarter Highlights (3-5 bullets)
What worked. Be specific.

## 6. Quarter Challenges (3-5 bullets)
What didn't work. Be honest. This is the most important slide.

## 7-9. Deep Dive Topics (3 slides max)
For each:
- The situation
- Options considered
- Your recommendation
- What you're asking from the board

## 10. Hiring
- Recent hires (with brief intro)
- Open roles + timeline
- Comp/equity flags if any

## 11. Financials
- P&L summary
- Cash position
- 18-month forecast (key assumptions)

## 12. Next Quarter Goals
- 3-5 specific, measurable goals

## 13. Discussion Topics
- Bullet list of things to discuss

## 14. Decisions Needed Today
- Specific decisions you need from the board (binary, with options)

## 15. Appendix
- Detailed metrics
- Customer case studies
- Competitor updates (if relevant)
