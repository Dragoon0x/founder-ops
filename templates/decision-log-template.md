# Decision Log Template

## Log Entry Format

### Decision: [Clear, specific title]
**Date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Decision Maker**: [Name and role]
**Status**: [Decided / Revisit by [date] / Reversed]

**Context**: What situation or opportunity prompted this decision? (2-3 sentences)

**Options Considered**:
1. [Option A]: [Description] — Pros: [x, y] / Cons: [x, y]
2. [Option B]: [Description] — Pros: [x, y] / Cons: [x, y]
3. [Option C]: [Description] — Pros: [x, y] / Cons: [x, y]

**Decision**: [Which option was chosen and why]

**Expected Outcome**: [What we expect to happen as a result]

**Review Date**: [When we'll evaluate if this was the right call]

**Actual Outcome**: [Filled in at review date]

---

## Why Keep a Decision Log
1. Institutional memory: new team members understand why things are the way they are
2. Learning: review past decisions to improve future ones
3. Accountability: decisions have owners
4. Speed: same decision doesn't get relitigated
5. Pattern recognition: spot decision-making biases over time

## Rules
- Log every decision that affects > 1 team or takes > $10K
- Don't log implementation details (how), only strategic choices (what and why)
- Review the log quarterly. Which decisions worked? Which didn't? Why?
