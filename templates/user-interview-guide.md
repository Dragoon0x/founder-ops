**Red flags to watch for during interview:**
- Am I leading them with my questions?
- Am I asking hypotheticals instead of past behavior?
- Am I pitching my solution instead of listening?

## Questions (15 max, don't ask all)

### About their world
- "Walk me through the last time you [specific task]"
- "What's the hardest part of [domain]?"
- "Who does [task] today? Walk me through what they do"

### About workarounds
- "What are you doing about [problem] today?"
- "What tools have you tried?"
- "What works about your current solution? What doesn't?"

### About motivation
- "Why did you start looking for a better way?"
- "What happens if this problem doesn't get solved?"
- "What would have to be true for you to change how you do this?"

### About buying
- "Who else would need to weigh in on a decision like this?"
- "How did you find [current solution] you use?"
- "What would change your mind about switching?"
