# Sales Call Script Template

## Discovery Call (45 minutes)

### Pre-call (5 min before)
- [ ] Review their LinkedIn
- [ ] Check their company news (last 30 days)
- [ ] Note 1-2 things to ask them about specifically

### Opening (5 min)
"Thanks for taking the time. Quick agenda check — I've got 45 minutes blocked.
I'd love to spend the first 30 understanding what's going on for you, and the
last 15 sharing how we might be able to help if it's a fit. Sound good?"

### Discovery (25 min)

**The Pain (open-ended):**
- "Walk me through what you're trying to solve."
- "What's the hardest part of [their domain]?"
- "What are you doing today about this?"

**The Cost:**
- "What does this cost you when it goes wrong?"
- "How much time does the team spend on this?"
- "What's the impact on [their business outcome]?"

**The Trigger:**
- "Why are you looking at solutions now vs 6 months ago?"
- "What changed?"

**The Decision:**
- "Who else is involved in solving this?"
- "What would success look like 6 months from now?"
- "What's your decision process typically?"

**The Alternatives:**
- "What other approaches are you considering?"
- "What's the alternative to fixing this?"

### Position (10 min)
Based on what you heard, position your fit:

"Based on what you described — [paraphrase their pain] — here's how we might
help. [Show specific feature/capability that addresses their specific pain].
The customers we work with who looked like you typically see [specific outcome
with metric]."

### Demo (only if they ask)
- Show 3 things max
- Show the things THEY mentioned, not your standard demo
- Avoid feature dumps

### Next Step (5 min)
"Based on what we discussed, here's what I'd suggest as a next step:

Option 1: [If clearly qualified] — Set up a technical deep dive with [stakeholder]
Option 2: [If exploring] — I'll send a one-pager and we revisit in [timeframe]
Option 3: [If not fit] — Honestly, I don't think we're the right fit because [reason]. Have you looked at [appropriate alternative]?"

## Follow-Up (within 4 hours)

```
Subject: [Their company] — recap of our call

[Name],

Thanks for the time today. Quick recap of what I heard:

The challenge:
- [Specific pain they described]
- [Specific impact]
- [Why now]

What success looks like:
- [Their outcome]
- [Their metric]
- [Their timeline]

How we'd approach it:
- [Specific to their situation, not generic]

Next step:
[Specific action with date]

Two things I promised to send:
1. [Resource]
2. [Resource]

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Common Mistakes
- Talking more than listening (target 70% them, 30% you)
- Pitching before understanding
- Skipping the trigger question
- Not asking about decision process
- Generic next steps ("let me know if you have questions")
- Not following up within 24 hours
- Sending generic decks before understanding
