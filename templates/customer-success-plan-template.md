## Review cadence

- **Weekly:** CSM notes in shared account doc
- **Monthly:** CSM + manager account review
- **Quarterly:** Cross-functional review (product feedback, finance, exec sponsor)

---

## Red flags to watch

- Primary champion leaves the company
- Usage drops >30% month-over-month
- Executive sponsor becomes unresponsive (>30 days no contact)
- Two or more support cases escalated in same quarter
- Business outcomes trending against target at month 6

Any of the above triggers a formal account review.
