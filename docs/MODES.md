# All 15 Modes — Detailed Reference

## Customer & Product

### discovery
**Use when:** Running customer interviews, synthesizing notes, finding patterns
**Output:** Pre-interview doc, post-interview synthesis, pattern reports
**Key framework:** The Mom Test + sequential hypothesis testing

### prioritize
**Use when:** Deciding what to build next, cutting scope
**Output:** RICE/ICE/MoSCoW scorecard + explicit kill list
**Key framework:** Score-then-cut approach; mandatory kill conversation

### product
**Use when:** Writing PRDs, user stories, technical decision records
**Output:** PRDs with kill criteria, ADRs, roadmaps (now/next/later)
**Key framework:** Pre-commit to kill criteria; decision reversibility

### positioning
**Use when:** Defining your market, ICP, value prop
**Output:** Positioning statement, ICP doc, competitive talk tracks
**Key framework:** April Dunford's 5-step positioning method

## Growth & Marketing

### growth
**Use when:** Designing experiments, evaluating channels, analyzing funnels
**Output:** Experiment briefs, channel scorecards, CAC/LTV analysis
**Key framework:** 70-point channel evaluation; 45+ threshold to test

### content
**Use when:** Writing blog posts, tweets, landing pages, newsletters, cold emails
**Output:** Full drafts with structure rules applied
**Key framework:** Voice rules (sounds like founder, not content marketer)

### community
**Use when:** Managing Discord/Slack, extracting user research, running AMAs
**Output:** Weekly community pulse, user research synthesis, AMA plans
**Key framework:** High-value vs low-value engagement patterns

## Fundraising & Finance

### fundraise
**Use when:** Researching investors, reviewing pitch decks, writing outreach
**Output:** Investor research briefs, deck reviews, cold email drafts
**Key framework:** Personalize to the partner, not the fund

### update
**Use when:** Writing monthly investor updates, board decks, team updates
**Output:** Investor update, board deck, all-hands update
**Key framework:** Lead with honesty; specific asks; consistent metrics

### finance
**Use when:** Modeling runway, experimenting with pricing, tracking unit economics
**Output:** Runway scenarios, pricing tests, unit economics dashboards
**Key framework:** Unit economics first, scale second

## People & Ops

### hire
**Use when:** Writing JDs, scoring candidates, designing interviews, checking references
**Output:** JDs, scorecards (85+/70-84/<70 ranges), interview kits, reference questions
**Key framework:** Work sample > resume screening; back-channel references

### calendar
**Use when:** Prepping for meetings, writing follow-ups, auditing calendar
**Output:** Meeting prep doc, follow-up email drafts, calendar audit report
**Key framework:** Meeting test (can this be an email/Loom/doc instead?)

### legal
**Use when:** Reviewing contracts, evaluating vendors, flagging legal issues
**Output:** Contract review with flags, vendor risk assessment
**Key framework:** 10-flag checklist per contract type; NOT legal advice

### compete
**Use when:** Researching competitors, mapping market, building talk tracks
**Output:** Competitor deep dives, market maps, positioning statements
**Key framework:** Monthly monitoring ritual; focus on customer outcomes

### retro
**Use when:** Weekly (5 min), monthly (30 min), or quarterly (1 hour) reflection
**Output:** Structured retro reports with metrics, learnings, next priorities
**Key framework:** Always end with "one thing to change"

## When to Use What

| Problem | Mode |
|---------|------|
| "I have 10 features to ship and only time for 3" | prioritize |
| "I need to raise $2M from seed investors" | fundraise |
| "My team is stuck and I'm not sure why" | retro |
| "I don't know what to build next" | discovery → prioritize |
| "We're running out of money" | finance |
| "This VC wants to chat" | fundraise → calendar |
| "Need to hire an engineer" | hire |
| "Our landing page isn't converting" | positioning → content |
| "I don't know if our prices are right" | finance (pricing experiments) |
| "I don't know who our real customer is" | discovery → positioning |
| "The team is asking for more autonomy" | product (principles + roadmap) |
| "A competitor just raised $50M" | compete |
| "Monthly update is due" | update |
