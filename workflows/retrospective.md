# Retrospective Workflow

## Trigger
Bi-weekly (end of sprint) or after any significant project/incident.

## Format Options

### Standard retro (45 min)
Best for regular sprint retros.

**Setup (5 min)**
- Each person writes on sticky notes (physical or virtual):
  - What went well (green)
  - What didn't go well (red)
  - Ideas for improvement (blue)
- 1 item per sticky. As many as you want. 3 min silent writing.

**Share & group (10 min)**
- Everyone puts up their stickies
- Facilitator groups similar items into themes
- Vote: each person gets 3 dots to place on the themes they most want to discuss

**Discuss (25 min)**
- Take the top 3 voted themes
- For each: what's the root cause? What can we do about it? Who owns the action?

**Close (5 min)**
- Read back action items with owners
- Quick round: "One word for how you feel about this sprint"

### 4Ls retro (45 min)
Best for project retros or after a major milestone.

Four categories:
- **Loved**: What made this project great?
- **Learned**: What did we discover (about our product, customers, or ourselves)?
- **Lacked**: What was missing that we needed?
- **Longed for**: What do we wish we had?

Same facilitation flow: silent writing → share → vote → discuss.

### Timeline retro (60 min)
Best for incident post-mortems or long projects.

- Draw a timeline on the wall (or Miro)
- Each person adds events they remember, placed chronologically
- Color code: green (positive), red (negative), yellow (turning points)
- Walk through the timeline together
- At each red/yellow moment: what would we do differently?

## Retro Anti-Patterns
1. **No actions**: Good discussion, nothing changes. Fix: every retro produces 2-3 actions with owners. Review last retro's actions at the start of the next one.
2. **Blame game**: "So-and-so messed up." Fix: blameless format, focus on systems not individuals.
3. **Same complaints**: The same issues come up every retro. Fix: if an action didn't work, try a different action. If the same problem persists for 3 retros, escalate it.
4. **Low energy**: People go through the motions. Fix: rotate facilitators, try different formats, keep it to 45 min.
5. **Missing voices**: Quiet people don't contribute. Fix: silent writing phase ensures everyone has input before group discussion.
