# Content Calendar Workflow

## Trigger
Weekly, every Monday morning.

## Weekly Planning (45 min)

### Step 1: Review last week (10 min)
Check performance of published content:
- Blog posts: views, time on page, conversion to signup
- Social media: impressions, engagement rate, link clicks
- Email: open rate, click rate, unsubscribe rate
- Any content that over-performed? Double down on that topic/format.
- Any content that flopped? Understand why before repeating the format.

### Step 2: This week's content slate (20 min)
Plan 5-7 pieces across channels:

| Day | Channel | Content Type | Topic | Owner |
|-----|---------|-------------|-------|-------|
| Mon | Blog | How-to guide | [Topic aligned with SEO target] | Content lead |
| Tue | Twitter | Thread | [Insight from customer conversations] | Founder |
| Wed | LinkedIn | Post | [Company building lesson] | Founder |
| Thu | Email | Newsletter | [Curated insights + product update] | Marketing |
| Fri | Twitter | Engagement | [Reply to industry conversations] | Marketing |

### Content type rotation
Don't publish the same format every week. Rotate:
- Educational (how-to, tutorial, guide)
- Thought leadership (opinion, insight, prediction)
- Social proof (customer story, case study, testimonial)
- Product (feature highlight, use case, demo)
- Culture (team story, hiring, behind-the-scenes)

### Step 3: Queue and schedule (15 min)
- Write or finalize drafts
- Schedule in publishing tool (Buffer, Hootsuite, or native scheduling)
- Set up tracking links (UTM parameters for blog posts and emails)

## Monthly Content Strategy Review (2 hrs)

### Metrics dashboard
| Metric | Target | Current |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Blog traffic (organic) | +10% MoM | |
| Email list growth | +5% MoM | |
| Social media followers | +3% MoM | |
| Content → signup conversion | > 2% | |
| Email open rate | > 25% | |
| Social engagement rate | > 3% | |

### Topic performance
Rank topics by engagement and conversion. Double down on top 3 topics, retire bottom 3.

### SEO check
- Review keyword rankings for target terms
- Identify new keyword opportunities from customer language
- Update 2-3 existing posts with fresh data and improved targeting
