# All-Hands Meeting Workflow

## Trigger
Monthly. Same day, same time. Consistency builds trust.

## Preparation (2 hrs, week before)

### Content collection
Request from each function lead (due 48 hrs before):
- Top metric + trend (1 number, 1 sentence)
- Biggest win (1 sentence, quantified)
- Biggest challenge (1 sentence, honest)
- Shoutout (1 team member who went above and beyond)

### Agenda construction
| Segment | Duration | Owner | Content |
|---------|----------|-------|---------|
| Opening | 5 min | CEO | Energy check, reminder of mission |
| Metrics dashboard | 10 min | CEO/COO | Key company metrics, trends, context |
| Function updates | 15 min | Leads | 3 min each, top-line only |
| Deep dive | 10 min | Rotating | One function presents something in depth |
| Q&A | 10 min | All | Anonymous question tool + live questions |
| Shoutouts | 5 min | All | Recognize great work |
| Closing | 5 min | CEO | What's ahead this month |

Total: 60 min. Never run over.

### Anonymous Q&A setup
Use a tool like Slido or Google Forms.
Open submissions 48 hrs before the meeting.
CEO reviews questions beforehand (not to filter, but to prepare thoughtful answers).
Answer the hard questions. Dodging them destroys trust faster than bad news does.

## During the Meeting

### Energy management
- Start with something human (welcome new hires, birthdays, personal milestones)
- Metrics first, then context. Numbers without narrative are confusing. Narrative without numbers is spin.
- Deep dive should teach something. Best format: "Here's a problem we faced, how we thought about it, what we did, what we learned."
- Q&A: read the hard questions out loud. Answer directly. "I don't know yet, here's when I'll have an answer" is a valid response.

### Recording
Record every all-hands for team members in different time zones or who are out.
Post recording + written summary within 24 hrs.

## Common All-Hands Problems
| Problem | Symptom | Fix |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Low attendance | < 70% of team shows up | Make it valuable. If people skip, your content isn't worth their time |
| No questions | Nobody submits or asks anything | Anonymous submissions + CEO models vulnerability by asking the first hard question |
| Too corporate | Feels like watching a quarterly earnings call | Cut the slides. Be conversational. Show the messy truth, not the polished version |
| Runs long | 60 min becomes 90 | Hard stop. No exceptions. Cut content, never cut the end time |
