## Stage Adjustments to Equity

Multiply Series A bands by:
- Pre-seed (no funding): 1.5-2.5x
- Seed: 1.2-1.8x
- Series A (baseline): 1.0x
- Series B: 0.5-0.7x
- Series C: 0.3-0.5x
- Pre-IPO: 0.1-0.3x
- Public: 0.05-0.15x (RSUs)

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Underpaying first 5 hires | Bad signal, hard to attract good ones |
| Equity-only at Series A+ | Top candidates pass |
| No formal bands until Series B | Inequity creates resentment |
| Comp benchmarking against FAANG | Wrong comparison; you're not them |
| No acceleration on acquisition | Top hires negotiate this |
| 4-year vesting without cliff | Industry standard is 1-year cliff |
| No refresh grants | Top hires leave at year 4 |
| Different bands for similar roles | Pay equity issues |
