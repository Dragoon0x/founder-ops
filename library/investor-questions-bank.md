# Investor Questions Bank

The questions investors ask, organized by topic and stage. Use this to
prep BEFORE meetings — write your answers in advance, refine over time.

## Round-Specific Questions

### Pre-Seed / Seed
- What's the founding insight? Why are you the team to do this?
- Who is your customer? How well do you know them?
- What's your unique unfair advantage?
- How big can this be?
- What have you built? What's working?
- What evidence do you have of demand?
- What are your unit economics indicating (even if early)?
- How are you spending the round?
- What's your 12-month milestone?
- Who else is in the round?

### Series A
- What's repeatable about your GTM?
- What are your retention curves?
- What's your magic number / sales efficiency?
- What's your CAC payback?
- Who is the ICP, and how clearly is it defined?
- What's your moat?
- What's your competitive landscape?
- Why now?
- What does the next 18 months look like?
- What's your hiring plan and burn projection?

### Series B
- What's the path to $100M ARR?
- What does the org need at scale?
- What's the international expansion path?
- What new products / markets are on the roadmap?
- What's your gross margin trajectory?
- How does net revenue retention compound?
- What's the leadership team look like?
- What's the path to profitability if needed?

### Series C+
- What's the IPO/M&A optionality?
- How are you operationally structured?
- What's the competitive position?
- What's the M&A strategy?
- How does the executive team look?
- What are the key risks at scale?

## Topic-Specific Questions

### Market & Customer
- Who is your customer, specifically?
- How many of them are there?
- What were they doing before you?
- Why do they buy from you specifically?
- What % of their workflow do you touch?
- What's the next adjacent customer?

### Product
- Walk me through a demo
- What's the user's "aha moment"?
- How long does it take to get there?
- What's the one feature your power users can't live without?
- What's the next 3 features on the roadmap?
- What's NOT on the roadmap and why?

### Traction
- Show me your monthly revenue chart
- Show me your cohort retention curves
- What % of new revenue is from existing customers?
- What's your top customer's spend trajectory?
- What's your churn rate, and why?
- What's your funnel conversion at each stage?

### Business Model
- How do you charge?
- What's the average contract value?
- What's the sales cycle?
- What's the gross margin?
- What's the contribution margin?
- How does this scale to $100M ARR?

### GTM
- Walk me through your sales process
- What's your CAC by channel?
- What's working that's repeatable?
- What's your sales team comp model?
- What does your pipeline look like?
- What's your win rate?

### Competition
- Who else does what you do?
- Why are you better than X?
- What happens when [BigCo] notices and copies you?
- What's your defensible moat?
- What if a customer asked you to bid against [Competitor]?

### Team
- Why this team for this market?
- What's the org plan?
- Who are the key hires next?
- What's your equity philosophy?
- How do you make decisions?
- What's your culture?

### Risks
- What are the top 3 risks?
- What's the biggest mistake you've made so far?
- What would have to be true for this to fail?
- What if [foundation model / regulation / competitor] changes?
- What if you can't raise the next round?

### Asks
- How much are you raising?
- What's the use of funds?
- What's the milestone you'll hit with this money?
- Who else is in the round?
- What's your timeline?
- What do you need from us beyond capital?

## Tough / Trick Questions

- "If you couldn't raise more money, what would you do?"
- "Why hasn't [BigCo] done this?"
- "What would make you sell the company tomorrow?"
- "What's the biggest weakness on your team?"
- "Tell me about a time you were wrong about something major"
- "If we passed, who would you go to next, and why?"
- "What would you do differently if you started over?"

## Questions YOU Should Ask Investors

Reverse the dynamic. These reveal a lot:

- What's your firm's thesis?
- What's the partnership decision process?
- How long does it take from term sheet to close?
- What other investments are in your portfolio similar to ours?
- How do you typically support founders post-investment?
- Have you led [stage] rounds before? When?
- What's the size of your current fund / how much do you have to deploy?
- How often do partners meet? When does our deal go to partnership?
- What would make you pass?
- What concerns do you have so far?
- Can I talk to one or two of your portfolio CEOs?

## Red Flags from Investor Questions

If you hear:
- "What's your TAM?" without follow-up = lazy
- "Just send me the deck" = not engaged
- "What's the lowest valuation you'd accept?" = not respecting you
- "We need exclusivity for 90 days" = unreasonable
- "We need a board observer" + "we need protective provisions" + "we need..." = trying to over-control
- "Let's just sign and figure out the docs later" = trying to skip diligence on their side too
