# Runway Extension Moves

When runway gets tight, you have more levers than you think. Ranked by impact-to-pain ratio.

## Quick wins (1-4 weeks)

1. **SaaS tool audit.** Cancel unused, downgrade tiers. Typical: 10-20% savings on tool spend. Effort: 1 week.
2. **Cloud right-sizing.** Reserved instances, spot, autoscaling, region shifts. Typical: 20-40% savings. Effort: 2-4 weeks.
3. **Annual-to-monthly for customers.** Offer discount for customers to prepay annually. Pulls 6-12 months of cash forward. Effort: 2 weeks outreach.
4. **AP stretching.** Move standard payment terms from net-30 to net-45 or net-60 with key vendors. No savings, just timing. Effort: 1 week.
5. **Travel freeze.** Zero out discretionary travel. Typical savings: 1-3% of budget. Effort: 1 day.

## Medium moves (1-3 months)

6. **Hiring freeze.** Don't backfill attrition, don't open new reqs. 3-6 month impact: 5-15% cost reduction.
7. **Contractor conversion.** Replace FTE with contractors (where feasible). Saves benefits + taxes. ~20% reduction in people cost for converted roles.
8. **Price increase.** 10-20% raise on new customers (or all customers at renewal). Direct revenue impact. Takes 3-6 months to fully show.
9. **Customer consolidation.** Encourage annual plans, multi-year contracts for discount. Cash upfront.
10. **Venture debt.** 6-18 month runway extension in exchange for warrants and covenants. Cost: ~10-15% IRR to lender. Requires existing equity runway as pledge.

## Hard moves (immediate but painful)

11. **Layoffs.** Cut one deep, not two shallow. 15-25% headcount cut typical for "emergency, not death." Extends runway 4-8 months. Damage: morale, reputation, legal complexity.
12. **Exec salary cuts.** CEO to $1 salary with equity refresh. VPs and execs -30-50%. Signals severity. Buys 2-4 months if cuts are meaningful.
13. **Sub-lease office.** If you have a lease. Months to execute. Saves 50-80% of office cost if you find tenant.
14. **Recap.** Cram-down round where existing investors get better terms in exchange for fresh cash. Destroys founder equity but keeps company alive. Last resort before shutdown.
15. **Secondary market sale.** Sell a piece of the company (asset sale, customer book) for cash.

## What NOT to do

- Freeze marketing completely (kills pipeline, compounds pain 3-6 months later)
- Stop sales hiring when you need revenue growth
- Delay R&D payments (poisons relationship with team)
- Skip payroll (catastrophic — legal, moral, trust)
- Lie to the board about runway (recoverable mistakes turn fatal)

## The math

Starting state: $2M cash, $300k/mo burn = 6.67 months runway.

Apply:
- Hiring freeze: -$50k/mo
- Cloud right-sizing: -$30k/mo
- SaaS audit: -$15k/mo
- Annual prepay incentive: +$400k one-time cash
- 15% headcount cut: -$70k/mo

New state: $2.4M cash, $135k/mo burn = 17.8 months runway.

Same company. 2.7x more runway. No magic. Just discipline.

## When to start

Trigger: runway < 12 months AND no confirmed next round.

Don't wait until 6 months. By then you're negotiating from weakness with vendors, employees, and investors.
