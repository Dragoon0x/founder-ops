# Signal: Founder Burnout Approaching

## What to Watch
- **Sleep**: < 6 hours consistently for 2+ weeks
- **Decision fatigue**: Avoiding decisions, deferring everything, analysis paralysis
- **Cynicism**: Negative reaction to good news ("It won't last")
- **Isolation**: Avoiding team interaction, canceling 1:1s
- **Physical symptoms**: Persistent fatigue, headaches, appetite changes
- **Performance**: Quality of decisions declining, more errors
- **Sunday dread**: Intense anxiety about the coming week, every week

## Why It Matters
Founder burnout is an existential risk to the company. A burned-out founder makes bad decisions, creates toxic culture, and eventually quits or implodes.

## Threshold for Action
3+ signals present for 2+ consecutive weeks.

## Recommended Response
1. **Immediate**: Take 2-3 days completely off. Not "working from home." Off.
2. **Short-term**: Delegate the bottom 50% of your task list (see founder-does-everything anti-pattern)
3. **Medium-term**: Hire or promote someone to take over one major function
4. **Ongoing**: Weekly protected time for non-work activities (exercise, friends, hobbies). Schedule it like a board meeting.
5. **Support**: Find a founder peer group, coach, or therapist. Not your co-founder or investor. Someone with no stake in the company.
