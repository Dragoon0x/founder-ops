# Signal: Product-Market Fit Approaching

## What to Watch
- **Organic growth**: > 30% of new users come from word of mouth or referral
- **Pull vs push**: Customers seek you out instead of you finding them
- **Retention curve flattens**: The % of users who stick levels off (doesn't keep declining)
- **NPS > 40**: Promoters significantly outnumber detractors
- **Usage frequency**: Users engage more often over time, not less
- **Pricing power**: You can raise prices without significant churn

## Why It Matters
PMF is the transition from "we think this might work" to "we know this works." Everything changes: hiring plan, spending plan, fundraising posture, competitive strategy.

## Threshold for Action
At least 3 of the 6 signals above must be present for 2+ consecutive months.

## Recommended Response
1. Document what's working: which customer segment, which use case, which value proposition
2. Begin scaling plan: hire for growth (sales, marketing, customer success)
3. Lock in the positioning that's resonating (don't change what's working)
4. Start fundraising conversations (you have leverage now)
5. Double down on the channels that are producing organic growth
