# Signal: Hiring Market Window Opening

## What to Watch
- **Tech layoffs**: Major companies announcing layoffs in your talent pool
- **Funding winter**: Startups in your space losing funding, reducing staff
- **Salary expectations**: Candidate salary asks decreasing or flattening
- **Application volume**: Inbound applications per posting increasing > 2x
- **Time to fill**: Decreasing without lowering quality bar
- **Offer acceptance rate**: Increasing

## Why It Matters
Talent availability is cyclical. In tight markets, it takes 6 months to fill a senior role. In loose markets, it takes 6 weeks. Hiring during a window can build a team in months that would take years otherwise.

## Threshold for Action
3+ signals present AND you have runway > 18 months.

## Recommended Response
1. **Accelerate critical hires**: Move approved-but-unfilled roles to top priority
2. **Upgrade underperformers**: In a talent-rich market, replace your weakest performers
3. **Build bench**: Hire promising candidates even if the role isn't open yet (if runway allows)
4. **Lock in talent**: Move quickly. Don't extend decision timelines. Good candidates still have options.
5. **Don't panic-hire**: Window doesn't mean lower your bar. It means more candidates meet your bar.
