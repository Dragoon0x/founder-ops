# Signal: Growth Channel Saturating

## What to Watch
- **CAC increasing**: Same channel, higher cost per acquisition for 3+ months
- **Conversion rate declining**: Same targeting, lower conversion
- **Diminishing returns on spend**: Doubling budget doesn't double results
- **Audience overlap**: Seeing the same people across campaigns
- **Frequency capping**: Reaching max impressions per user
- **Response rates declining**: Outbound email/LinkedIn response rates dropping

## Why It Matters
Every growth channel has a ceiling. Companies that depend on one channel are fragile. When your primary channel saturates, growth stalls unless you have alternatives ready.

## Threshold for Action
Primary channel CAC increased > 30% over 6 months while conversion declined > 20%.

## Recommended Response
1. **Diversify**: Test 2-3 new channels simultaneously (allocate 20% of budget to experiments)
2. **Optimize existing**: Before abandoning, try: new creative, new targeting, different offers
3. **Build owned channels**: Email list, community, content library. These don't saturate the same way.
4. **Referral program**: Your best customers are your best channel. Systematize it.
5. **Product-led growth**: If you're purely sales-led, adding a self-serve motion opens a new channel
