# Signal: Market is Shifting

## What to Watch
- **Customer language**: Prospects use different words to describe their problem than 6 months ago
- **New entrants**: 3+ new competitors appeared in the last year
- **Existing competitor pivots**: A major competitor changes positioning or pricing
- **Adjacent technology**: A platform shift is happening (AI, mobile-first, API-first)
- **Regulatory changes**: New laws or regulations affect your market
- **Customer buying behavior**: Decision-maker role is changing, procurement process is different
- **Budget shifts**: Customers' budgets are moving between departments

## Why It Matters
Markets don't announce when they shift. By the time it's obvious, the window to respond has closed.

## Threshold for Action
2+ signals present simultaneously.

## Recommended Response
1. **Customer research sprint**: 10 customer interviews in 2 weeks, focused on: "How is your world changing?"
2. **Competitive landscape update**: Full market map refresh
3. **Positioning review**: Does our current positioning still resonate with where the market is going?
4. **Scenario planning**: "If this shift continues, what does our business look like in 12 months?"
5. **Place a bet**: Don't wait for certainty. Pick a direction and move. You can course-correct, but standing still is the worst option.
