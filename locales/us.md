# United States

## Business culture (high signal things outsiders miss)
- Directness varies by coast. East Coast (NYC, Boston) is blunter; West Coast (SF, LA) leads with warmth then delivers hard feedback softer. Both expect speed.
- "Let's circle back" often means no. "That's interesting" often means no. "Send me a deck" means yes, but you have ~10 days before it goes stale.
- Networking is transactional but warmth-coded. Offer something (a stat, an intro, a take) before asking for anything.

## Hiring
- At-will employment in every state except Montana. Can terminate for any non-protected reason.
- California, New York, Colorado, Washington require salary range disclosure on job posts. Comply or face fines ($1K-$10K per violation in some states).
- Non-competes unenforceable in California, Minnesota, North Dakota, Oklahoma, and partially elsewhere. FTC 2024 rule banning them is in legal limbo as of 2026. Assume non-competes won't hold; use non-solicit + IP assignment instead.
- Classification: 1099 contractors vs W-2 employees. Misclassification penalties: back taxes + 20% penalty + interest. Use ABC test in CA/NJ/MA (stricter than federal).
- Healthcare: required to offer if 50+ FTEs (ACA employer mandate). Below 50, it's optional but expected for knowledge workers.

## Fundraising
- Typical angel check: $10K-$50K. Seed: $500K-$3M in SAFEs/convertibles is the norm. Series A: $3M-$15M priced round.
- SAFEs (YC post-money) are the default pre-seed/seed instrument. 20% discount + $8-15M cap typical for pre-seed; $15-30M cap typical for seed.
- Delaware C-Corp is the standard incorporation. Don't incorporate in California — franchise tax plus worse investor comfort.
- 409A valuation required before first option grant. Costs $2K-$5K per year. 409A is ~25-35% of preferred price typically.
- Blue Sky filings: Form D within 15 days of first sale. State filings vary.

## Taxes
- Federal corporate rate: 21%. State corporate taxes layered on top (CA: 8.84%, NY: 7.25%, TX/NV/WY/FL: 0%).
- R&D tax credit: up to $500K/year offset against payroll tax for qualified small businesses. Worth claiming from year 1.
- Section 1202 QSBS: 100% capital gains exclusion on C-corp stock held 5+ years, up to $10M or 10x basis. Critical for founder/employee equity.

## Customer expectations (B2B SaaS)
- Monthly billing expected at SMB. Annual with discount (10-20%) at mid-market. Multi-year at enterprise.
- Net 30 payment terms standard for enterprise. Net 15 for SMB. Upfront credit card for self-serve.
- SOC 2 Type II required to sell above $50K ACV in most verticals by 2026.
- Procurement cycles: SMB 1-4 weeks, mid-market 6-12 weeks, enterprise 3-9 months.

## Foot-guns
- Using your personal bank account for company spend. Mix funds = pierce the corporate veil = personal liability.
- Verbal equity promises to employees/advisors. Always paper the grant within 30 days of start date.
- Hiring W-2 in a state you're not registered in. Triggers nexus, payroll tax registration, unemployment insurance accounts. Each state is a separate registration.
- Using stock photos of employees on the site when you don't have them yet. Class-action bait in CA.
