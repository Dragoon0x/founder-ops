# Locales

Cultural, linguistic, and regulatory context for operating across regions. Each locale file covers: how investors behave, how employees expect to be managed, hiring norms, legal basics, pricing expectations, and common foot-guns for outsiders.

Not legal advice. Not a replacement for local counsel. Use as a starting frame.

## Files
- `us.md` — United States baseline
- `uk-ireland.md` — UK and Ireland
- `eu-continental.md` — Germany, France, Netherlands, Nordics
- `india.md` — India (startups + enterprise)
- `southeast-asia.md` — Singapore, Indonesia, Vietnam, Philippines
- `latam.md` — Mexico, Brazil, Colombia, Argentina
- `mena.md` — UAE, Saudi Arabia, Egypt
- `japan-korea.md` — Japan and South Korea
- `australia-nz.md` — Australia and New Zealand
- `canada.md` — Canada

## When to read
Before hiring your first person in a region, before fundraising from regional investors, before signing your first enterprise customer there, before setting pricing for that market.
