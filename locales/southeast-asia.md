# Southeast Asia (Singapore, Indonesia, Vietnam, Philippines)

## Singapore
- Default regional HQ. Pte Ltd is standard. $1 minimum capital.
- Must have 1 local resident director (citizen/PR/EP holder). Common to use a nominee service.
- Corporate tax: 17% with exemptions — new startup exemption: 75% on first S$100K, 50% on next S$100K for first 3 years.
- GST: 9% (from 2024). Must register > S$1M turnover.
- Employment: 2 weeks notice during probation, 1 month after, more senior roles longer. CPF employer contribution 17% for citizens/PRs.
- Work visa: Employment Pass requires min S$5,600/month (rising with age/experience), plus skills assessment COMPASS.
- Strong IP regime, efficient courts, arbitration hub.

## Indonesia
- PT PMA (foreign-owned) is the standard foreign entity. Capital requirement IDR 10B (~$650K) investment plan.
- Business licenses via OSS (Online Single Submission). Negative Investment List restricts certain sectors.
- VAT: 11% (rising to 12%). Income tax 22%.
- Labor: 13th month salary (THR) mandatory, paid before Eid. Severance calculations complex and generous — max can reach 32 months salary.
- Culturally: relationship-heavy, hierarchical, meeting-heavy. Slower decisions but strong once made.

## Vietnam
- Foreign-invested enterprise (FIE) setup. 2-6 months typical.
- Corporate tax: 20% standard, 10% for encouraged sectors.
- Labor law pro-employee. Severance 1/2 month per year worked if > 1 year tenure.
- Hanoi vs HCMC: government/enterprise leans Hanoi, tech/startups lean HCMC.
- Banking and forex can be friction-heavy. Work with local bank that understands tech.

## Philippines
- PEZA-registered entities get tax holidays and incentives. Non-PEZA: 25% corporate tax.
- 13th month pay mandatory by law. Separation pay 1 month salary per year for authorized cause.
- English proficiency high — major BPO base. Time zone overlaps well with US West Coast evenings.
- Customs and import can be slow. Avoid if you're shipping physical product.

## Cross-cutting
- Hofstede power distance high in ID/VN/PH — flat team cultures need active cultivation.
- English: SG fluent, PH fluent, ID/VN varies — senior roles usually fine, mid-level check carefully.
- Sales: enterprise is relationship + top-down. Bottom-up PLG works but slower than US.
