# End of Year Checklist

Annual wrap-up for reflection, closure, and setup for next year.

## Early December

### Financials
- [ ] Year-end financial close schedule confirmed
- [ ] Accounts reconciled through end of Q3
- [ ] Revenue recognition reviewed (if complex)
- [ ] Tax accountant engaged

### Compliance
- [ ] Annual state filings (registered states, franchise taxes)
- [ ] Delaware franchise tax (if incorporated there)
- [ ] W-2 / 1099 prep started
- [ ] Employee benefit elections for next year open

## Mid-December

### Personnel
- [ ] Performance reviews completed (or scheduled for January)
- [ ] Compensation changes drafted for Jan 1
- [ ] Equity grants for annual refresh calculated
- [ ] Bonus calculations (if applicable)

### Strategic
- [ ] Annual retrospective scheduled (whole company, 2-3 hours)
- [ ] Year-end investor update drafted
- [ ] 2026 annual plan in progress

## Late December

### Financials
- [ ] Q4 close started
- [ ] Final invoices out to customers
- [ ] Vendor invoices collected
- [ ] 1099 information collected from contractors

### People
- [ ] Holiday time off policy communicated
- [ ] Holiday card / message (if applicable)
- [ ] Team recognition / awards moments
- [ ] Handoff plans for anyone OOO

### Operational
- [ ] Renewals for critical tools / vendors reviewed
- [ ] Unused tool subscriptions cancelled (typical 10-20% savings)
- [ ] Insurance policies reviewed
- [ ] Contracts expiring in Q1 next year listed

## Final Week of Year

### Reflection (half-day for founder)
- [ ] Annual metrics review (vs. Jan 1 plan)
- [ ] Personal reflection: what did YOU do this year
- [ ] Team retrospective day
- [ ] Founder journal entry (private, for future reference)

### Setup for New Year
- [ ] 2026 OKRs draft
- [ ] Q1 plan drafted
- [ ] First-week-of-year kickoff meeting on calendar
- [ ] Holiday coverage / on-call for emergencies

## First Week of January

### Kickoff
- [ ] All-hands kickoff (theme for year, not just tactics)
- [ ] Compensation changes effective
- [ ] Equity grants executed (board approval if applicable)
- [ ] 2026 plan launched

### Compliance
- [ ] W-2s / 1099s sent by Jan 31 (legal requirement US)
- [ ] State filings as required
- [ ] Annual report to investors sent

### Reset
- [ ] Quarterly planning for Q1 (see `checklists/quarterly-planning-checklist.md`)
- [ ] Dashboards reset / archived
- [ ] OKR system refreshed
- [ ] Annual retro lessons applied

## What to NOT Do Around End of Year

- [ ] Launch major products between Dec 15 and Jan 15 (low attention)
- [ ] Do annual reviews in first week of January (rush it = poor quality)
- [ ] Miss key compliance deadlines (fines are real)
- [ ] Skip the reflection (compound learning happens here)

## The Founder's Private Year-End Review

Block 2 hours, solo. Questions:

1. What's the ONE thing I did this year I'm most proud of?
2. What's the ONE thing I wish I could redo?
3. Who on the team do I owe a specific thank-you to?
4. What habit served me best this year?
5. What habit hurt me most?
6. What did I believe in January that I no longer believe?
7. What will I do differently next year?

Write these down. Read them next December.
