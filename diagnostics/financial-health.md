# Diagnostic: Financial Health

Run monthly (quick) and quarterly (deep).

## Quick Monthly Check (15 min)

| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Runway | > 18 months | 9-18 months | < 9 months |
| Burn multiple | < 2x | 2-3x | > 3x |
| Revenue growth (MoM) | > 10% | 5-10% | < 5% |
| Gross margin | > 75% | 60-75% | < 60% |
| Cash balance trend | Stable/growing | Slow decline | Rapid decline |

If any metric is Red: trigger detailed financial review within 1 week.

## Quarterly Deep Dive (3 hrs)

### Unit Economics
- CAC by channel (trending up or down?)
- LTV by segment (which customers are most valuable?)
- LTV/CAC ratio (above 3:1 overall? Per segment?)
- Payback period (within target for your GTM motion?)
- Gross margin by product/plan (any money-losing tiers?)

### Revenue Quality
- MRR breakdown: new, expansion, contraction, churn
- Net revenue retention: above 100%? Trend?
- Revenue concentration: top 10 customers as % of total ARR
- Contract length distribution: monthly vs annual vs multi-year
- ARR per employee: target > $100K at Series A, > $200K at Series B

### Cash Management
- 13-week cash flow forecast accuracy vs actuals
- Days sales outstanding (DSO): target < 45 days
- Deferred revenue balance and trend
- Upcoming large expenses (hiring, infrastructure, legal)

### Investor Readiness
- Metrics in shape for next round?
- Cap table clean? (no messy convertible notes, SAFEs accounted for)
- Financial model updated with current assumptions?
- Board reporting on schedule?

## Scoring
Each section gets a Green/Yellow/Red assessment.
- All Green: financially healthy, focus on growth
- 1-2 Yellow: monitor closely, build improvement plans
- Any Red: immediate CEO attention, action plan within 1 week
- 2+ Red: financial review with board, consider bridge or cost reduction
