# Diagnostic: Go-to-Market Health

Run quarterly. Marketing + Sales + CS leaders together.

## Funnel Metrics

### Awareness → Interest
- Website traffic growth: target > 10% MoM
- Organic traffic as % of total: target > 40%
- Content engagement rate: target > 3%
- Brand search volume trend: increasing

### Interest → Consideration
- Lead-to-MQL conversion: target > 15%
- MQL-to-SQL conversion: target > 30%
- Demo request rate: target > 5% of website visitors
- Free trial signup rate: target > 2% of visitors (PLG)

### Consideration → Purchase
- SQL-to-opportunity conversion: target > 40%
- Win rate: target > 25%
- Sales cycle length: within target for segment
- Average deal size: stable or increasing

### Purchase → Expansion
- Onboarding completion rate: target > 80%
- Time to value: < 14 days
- NPS at 30 days: target > 40
- Expansion revenue as % of new: target > 30%

## Qualitative Assessment
Rate 1-5:
- ICP clarity: Can every team member describe your ideal customer in 30 seconds?
- Positioning differentiation: Can prospects articulate why you're different from alternatives?
- Sales-marketing alignment: Is marketing generating leads that sales wants to work?
- Competitive clarity: Does the team know how to win against each major competitor?
- Customer story bank: Do you have 10+ customer stories you can use in sales and marketing?

## Scoring
Add funnel scores (each stage 1-5 based on distance from target) + qualitative (1-5 each).
- > 40: Strong GTM machine. Optimize and scale.
- 30-40: Good foundation. Fix the weakest funnel stage.
- 20-30: Gaps present. Prioritize ICP clarity and positioning before spending more on channels.
- < 20: Rebuild. Your GTM fundamentals need work before channel investment.
