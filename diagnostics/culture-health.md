# Diagnostic: Culture Health

Run bi-annually. Anonymous survey + leadership assessment.

## Survey Questions (1-5 scale)

### Psychological Safety
1. I feel safe taking risks on this team
2. I can bring up problems and tough issues
3. People on this team don't reject others for being different
4. It's safe to ask for help
5. No one on this team would deliberately undermine my efforts

### Clarity & Alignment
6. I understand the company's top priorities
7. I know how my work connects to company goals
8. Decision-making processes are clear
9. I get the information I need to do my job well
10. Leadership communicates openly about challenges

### Growth & Development
11. I'm learning and growing in my role
12. My manager invests in my development
13. I see a path for advancement here
14. I receive regular, useful feedback
15. My strengths are utilized

### Belonging & Inclusion
16. I feel like I belong here
17. I can be my authentic self at work
18. Diverse perspectives are valued and sought
19. I would recommend this company to a friend
20. I see myself here in 12 months

## Scoring
Average score per category:
- 4.5-5.0: Exceptional. Your culture is a competitive advantage.
- 4.0-4.4: Strong. Address specific low-scoring items.
- 3.5-3.9: Average. Proactive investment needed.
- 3.0-3.4: Concerning. Multiple systemic issues.
- < 3.0: Critical. Culture is a retention risk.

## Response Protocol
- Share results transparently with the whole company
- Identify top 3 lowest-scoring items
- Create action plan for each (owner + timeline + measurable target)
- Follow up in 90 days with progress update
- Re-survey at 6 months

## Red Flags in Open-Ended Responses
- "Management doesn't listen"
- "Things change too often"
- "I don't know what success looks like"
- "Some people are treated differently"
- "I'm looking for other opportunities"

Any of these appearing in > 15% of responses is a systemic issue.
