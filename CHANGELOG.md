# Changelog

## 1.0.0 — Initial Release

**15 specialist modes covering the complete founder workflow:**

**Customer & Product:**
- discovery — Customer discovery interviews (prep, synthesis, patterns)
- prioritize — Feature prioritization with RICE/ICE/MoSCoW
- product — PRDs, user stories, ADRs, roadmaps
- positioning — ICP, messaging, value prop testing

**Growth & Marketing:**
- growth — Channel experiments, A/B tests, CAC/LTV
- content — Blog posts, tweets, landing pages, newsletters
- community — User research, engagement patterns

**Fundraising & Finance:**
- fundraise — Investor research, pitch decks, outreach
- update — Investor/board updates
- finance — Runway, pricing, unit economics

**People & Ops:**
- hire — JDs, candidate scoring, interview kits
- calendar — Meeting prep, follow-ups
- legal — Contract flags, vendor eval
- compete — Competitive intelligence
- retro — Weekly/monthly/quarterly retros

**Infrastructure:**
- CLAUDE.md with core principles
- SKILL.md as progressive disclosure entry
- Shared context (`_shared.md`) loaded by every mode
- 4 config templates (founder, company, product, investors)
- MIT LICENSE
- DISCLAIMER.md covering legal/financial/investment disclaimers
- Zero dependencies — pure Markdown skills
