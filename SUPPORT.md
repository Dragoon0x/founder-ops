## Response Time

This is a community project. Realistic expectations:
- Critical bugs: 48-72 hours
- Feature requests: No SLA
- Questions: Community-driven (faster response if clear + researched)

## Commercial Support

There is no commercial support. This is a free community project.

If you need deeper help:
- Hire a fractional COO or operator who can implement these workflows
- Work with a startup coach (Reboot.io, Conscious Leadership, etc.)
- Post specific questions with context in GitHub Discussions

## What's Out of Scope

We can't help with:
- Your specific business decisions (we give frameworks, not verdicts)
- Legal advice (talk to a lawyer)
- Financial advice (talk to a CPA)
- Investment advice (talk to an advisor)
- Hiring specific people (we give methodology, not candidates)
- Debugging your Claude Code setup (ask Anthropic)

## Feedback

Most valuable feedback:
- "I used mode X in situation Y and got result Z"
- "Mode X missed consideration Y"
- "Framework X applied to Y scenario produced Z insight"
- "This playbook saved me N hours on situation Y"

Post in GitHub Discussions under "Show and tell" or "Feedback".
