# Mode: Go-to-Market Strategy

## Purpose

Build a coherent GTM strategy: who you sell to, how you reach them, how you
convert them, how you keep them. Stop running 5 disconnected experiments.

## The 7 Pillars of GTM

```
1. ICP (who)              ─┐
2. Positioning (why)       ├─→ Messaging (what to say)
3. Buyer journey (how)    ─┘
4. Channels (where)        ├─→ Tactics (specific actions)
5. Sales motion (how sold) ┘
6. Pricing & packaging
7. Onboarding & expansion
```

If any pillar is broken, GTM stalls. Diagnose by pillar, fix in order.

## Sales Motion Decision Tree

| Annual Contract Value (ACV) | Sales Motion | Cycle | Org Required |
|------|----------|-----|--------|
| $0-$1K | Self-serve / PLG | 0-7 days | Marketing + product, no sales |
| $1K-$10K | Self-serve + assist | 7-30 days | 1-2 SDR/AE hybrids |
| $10K-$50K | Inside sales | 30-90 days | AE team, light SDR |
| $50K-$250K | Mid-market sales | 90-180 days | AE + SDR + SE |
| $250K-$1M | Enterprise sales | 180-365 days | AE + SDR + SE + CSM + Legal |
| $1M+ | Strategic accounts | 365-730 days | Named accounts, executive sponsor |

**Rule:** ACV must be 5-10x of fully-loaded cost of the salesperson per deal.
A $150K-loaded AE needs to close $750K-$1.5M in ARR/year minimum.

## GTM Maturity Model

| Stage | ARR | GTM State | Focus |
|-------|-----|-----------|-------|
| Founder-led | $0-$1M | Founder closes everything | Find ICP, validate willingness to pay |
| Repeatable | $1M-$3M | First sales hire, founder still 50%+ | Document playbook, replicable wins |
| Scalable | $3M-$10M | Sales team of 3-8, founder <20% | Quota attainment, predictable pipeline |
| Efficient | $10M-$50M | Specialized team, marketing engine | Magic number, payback period |
| Optimized | $50M+ | Multiple segments, geographies | Net retention, expansion revenue |

**Most companies plateau at the wrong stage.** Diagnostic: if your sales cycle,
deal size, or close rate hasn't improved in 12 months, you're stuck.

## GTM Playbook Document (write this)

```
## [Company] GTM Playbook v[N]

### Who we sell to (ICP)
- Firmographic: [size/industry/geo/revenue]
- Technographic: [stack signals]
- Trigger events: [hiring sprees / fundraising / product launches]
- Disqualifiers: [explicit non-ICP]

### What we sell (positioning)
- Category: [the box we put ourselves in]
- Primary alternative: [what they use today]
- Unique value: [one differentiated benefit]

### How we reach them (channels)
- Primary: [channel doing 60%+ of pipeline]
- Secondary: [channel doing 20-30%]
- Experimental: [3-month tests]

### How we sell (process)
1. [Source] — SDR books meeting
2. Discovery call (30 min) — qualify with [framework]
3. Demo (45 min) — show value with [customer scenario]
4. Proposal — pricing tier + terms
5. Close — verbal commit, then paper

### Average metrics
- Lead → Meeting: [%]
- Meeting → Opp: [%]
- Opp → Close: [%]
- Sales cycle: [days]
- ACV: $[X]
- CAC: $[X]
- Payback: [months]

### What we're testing this quarter
1. [Specific experiment]
2. [Specific experiment]
```
