# Mode: Advisors

## Purpose

Recruit, structure, and manage advisors. Most founders either have too
many useless advisors or rely on a few overburdened ones.

## When to Add an Advisor

Add an advisor when:
- You have a SPECIFIC gap in expertise (not "general business advice")
- The gap will limit you for the next 6-12 months
- You can articulate what success with this advisor looks like
- You'll actually use them (talk monthly minimum)

DON'T add an advisor when:
- You want their logo on your website
- You hope they'll make intros (they won't if you don't engage)
- You're collecting names instead of expertise
- You don't have specific work for them

## The Ideal Advisor Profile

| Trait | Why it matters |
|-------|---------------|
| Operating experience in your stage | Pattern recognition that matters |
| Direct experience with your specific problem | Actionable advice, not theory |
| 5-15 year head start on your problem | Not so far ahead they've forgotten |
| 2-4 other advisor commitments max | Not over-extended |
| Responds within 48 hours | They actually care |
| Will push back, not just agree | Truth-teller |
| You'd hire them as an exec if you could | Quality bar |

## Advisor Outreach

**Cold outreach (under 150 words):**
```
Subject: [Specific shared connection or relevant work they did]

[Name],

I read [specific thing they wrote / shipped / said]. Specifically the
[specific insight] resonated with what we're working on at [Company].

Quick context: We're [one line about company + traction signal].
We're at the point where [specific challenge that they have direct
experience with].

I'd love to explore whether you have the bandwidth to advise us on
[specific topic]. Specifically:
- [Specific commitment: 1 hour/month, async questions]
- [Specific topic area]
- [Compensation: 0.1-0.5% over 4 years standard]

15 minutes to discuss?

[Name]
[One credibility line]
```

## Advisor Compensation

**Standard equity grants (US, early stage):**

| Stage of company | Advisor commitment | Equity range |
|-----------------|-------------------|-------------|
| Idea stage | Monthly call + intros | 0.5 – 1.0% |
| Pre-seed | Monthly call + specific work | 0.25 – 0.5% |
| Seed | Monthly call | 0.1 – 0.25% |
| Series A | Monthly call | 0.05 – 0.1% |
| Series B+ | Mostly cash + small equity | 0.025 – 0.05% |

**Vesting structure:**
- 4 years, 1 year cliff (standard) OR
- 2 years, no cliff (more advisor-friendly)
- Acceleration on acquisition: typical

**Use Founder Advisor Standard Template (FAST) agreement** from Founder
Institute as starting point — covers the basics fairly.

## The Advisor Agreement

Specific commitments to document:

```
## Advisor Agreement — [Name] × [Company]

### Term
[Start date] – [End date or until terminated]

### Time commitment
- [N] hours per month
- Available for [type of engagement]: calls, async, reviews, intros

### Topic scope
- Primary: [specific area they advise on]
- Secondary: [other areas if asked]
- Out of scope: [things they're NOT signing up for]

### Compensation
- Equity: [X]% of fully diluted shares
- Vesting: [terms]
- Cliff: [terms]
- Acceleration: [terms]

### Mutual expectations
- Founder: prepares agendas, respects time, follows up on advice
- Advisor: responds within [N] days, gives honest feedback, declines if no bandwidth

### Exclusivity / conflict
- Advisor will not advise direct competitors
- Founder will not list advisor publicly without permission

### Termination
Either party can end with 30 days notice. Vested equity retained.
```

## Working With Advisors Effectively

### The Monthly Advisor Sync

**Structure (30-45 min):**
```
0-5 min  — What's changed (highlights, lowlights, key metrics)
5-25 min — One specific topic / question for them
25-35 min — Their feedback / advice
35-40 min — Action items from this conversation
40-45 min — What I need from them before next sync
```

**Rules:**
- Send agenda 24+ hours before
- ONE topic, not five (focus = useful advice)
- Take notes, share back within 24 hours
- Follow up on previous advice (did you do it? what happened?)

### Async Advisor Questions

For quick questions outside monthly calls:
```
Subject: Quick advice — [specific topic]

[Name],

Quick context: [2-3 sentences]
Specific question: [1 sentence]
Why it matters now: [1 sentence]
What I'm leaning toward: [1 sentence]
Decision deadline: [date]

If you have 5 minutes to react, I'd appreciate it. No pressure if not.

[Name]
```

## Advisor Anti-Patterns

| Anti-pattern | Problem | Fix |
|--------------|---------|-----|
| Too many advisors | Each gets little attention | Maximum 5 active advisors |
| Logo collection | Useless to actual operations | Quality > quantity |
| Vague engagement | Nothing happens | Specific monthly commitment |
| One-way information flow | Advisor can't help | Send updates between syncs |
| No follow-through on advice | Advisor disengages | Report back: what you did + result |
| Avoiding hard questions | Wasted opportunity | Bring your toughest issues to advisors |
| Advisor as therapist | Burns them out | Use peer founders for emotional support |
