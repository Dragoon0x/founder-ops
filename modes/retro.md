# Mode: Retrospectives

## Purpose

Weekly and monthly retrospectives that actually change behavior. Not
generic "what went well / what didn't" — structured reflection that
produces changes.

## Weekly Retro (5 minutes, every Friday)

```
## Week of [date]

### Shipped
[Specific things that went live or were completed]

### Didn't Ship (and why)
[Things planned but not completed — causes]

### Metrics Update
| Metric | Start | End | Change |
|--------|-------|-----|--------|
| [key metric] | X | Y | +/- |

### Top 3 Learnings
1. [Specific, actionable learning]
2. [Specific, actionable learning]
3. [Specific, actionable learning]

### Energy / Focus Check
- Energy (1-10): [score]
- Focus (1-10): [score]
- Biggest drain this week: [what]
- Biggest win this week: [what]

### Next Week Priorities (top 3, no more)
1. [Specific, measurable]
2. [Specific, measurable]
3. [Specific, measurable]

### One Change
[One specific thing I'll do differently next week]
```

## Monthly Retro (30 minutes, last day of month)

```
## Month of [Month Year]

### Major Shipments
[Big things that went live]

### Goals vs Actual
| Goal | Target | Actual | Status |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| [goal] | X | Y | ✓/✗ |

### Metrics Month-over-Month
| Metric | Last Month | This Month | Change |
|--------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| MRR | $X | $Y | +Z% |
| Customers | X | Y | +Z |
| Churn | X% | Y% | +/- |

### What Worked
[3-5 specific things, with WHY they worked]

### What Didn't Work
[3-5 specific things, with WHY they didn't]

### Surprises
[Things that happened that we didn't predict — positive or negative]

### Decisions Made This Month
[Major decisions with status: did they turn out well?]

### Am I Spending Time on the Right Things?
- [Look at where time went]
- [Was it on the right priorities?]
- [What should shift next month?]

### Next Month Focus (3 priorities)
1. [Specific goal with measurable target]
2. [Specific goal with measurable target]
3. [Specific goal with measurable target]

### One Thing to Stop Doing
[Something from this month that isn't worth continuing]

### One Thing to Start Doing
[Something new to try based on learnings]

### One Thing to Continue
[Something working that needs reinforcement]
```

## Quarterly Retro (1 hour, end of quarter)

Include monthly components plus:

```
### Strategic Review
- Are we still building for the right customer?
- Is our positioning still right?
- What would we do differently if we started this quarter over?

### Big Picture Metrics
- ARR: $[X] (target: $[Y])
- Customers: [X] (target: [Y])
- Burn: $[X]/mo (runway: [Z] months)
- Team: [X] people

### Wins to Celebrate
[Big things worth acknowledging]

### Decisions to Revisit
[Decisions from last quarter — were they right?]

### Biggest Risks Going Forward
1. [Risk] — [mitigation]
2. [Risk] — [mitigation]
3. [Risk] — [mitigation]

### Next Quarter North Star
[One sentence: what does success look like in 90 days?]

### Supporting Quarterly Goals (3-5)
1. [Specific, measurable]
2. [Specific, measurable]
3. [Specific, measurable]
```

## Retro Anti-Patterns

| Pattern | Problem | Fix |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Skipping when busy | Most important when busy | 5 min is enough |
| Generic "went well / didn't" | No change results | Specific + why |
| No follow-through on changes | Same problems recur | Track "one change" weekly |
| Self-critical only | Burnout fuel | Equal time on what worked |
| No action items | Feels good, changes nothing | Every retro has ≥1 specific change |

## Output

- `reports/retro-week-[date].md`
- `reports/retro-month-[month-year].md`
- `reports/retro-quarter-[Q-year].md`

## The Compounding Effect

5 minutes/week for 52 weeks = 4.3 hours/year
That 4.3 hours produces:
- 52 "one thing to change" commitments
- Trend data on your energy, focus, velocity
- A running log of decisions and their outcomes
- Early warning signals before problems compound

Skipping retros is the #1 way founders drift off course.
