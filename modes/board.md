# Mode: Board Management

## Purpose

Run boards that produce decisions, not just updates. Manage board members
between meetings. Build the board you need at each stage.

## Submodes

### 1. Board Composition by Stage

| Stage | Board Size | Composition |
|-------|------------|-------------|
| Pre-seed | 2-3 | Founders only, maybe 1 advisor |
| Seed | 3-4 | 2 founders + 1-2 lead investors |
| Series A | 4-5 | 2 founders + 2 investors + 1 independent |
| Series B+ | 5-7 | 1-2 founders + 2-3 investors + 2-3 independents |

**Independent directors are the most underrated.** They have no economic
agenda. They tell you the truth. Add the first one as soon as you raise
Series A — ideally before.

### 2. Board Meeting Cadence

| Stage | Frequency | Length | Format |
|-------|-----------|--------|--------|
| Pre-seed | Quarterly | 60 min | Video, informal |
| Seed | Quarterly | 90 min | Video or in-person |
| Series A | Every 6 weeks | 2 hours | Mostly in-person |
| Series B+ | Monthly or every 6 weeks | 2-3 hours | In-person preferred |

### 3. Board Pack (sent 48-72 hours before)

Length: 15-25 slides. Pre-read mandatory.

```
1. Cover + agenda
2. CEO summary (1 slide, "what matters this period")
3. Key metrics dashboard (1 slide)
4. Operational deep dive 1 (biggest opportunity OR risk)
5-6. Operational deep dive 2 (the other one)
7. Financials (P&L, cash flow, runway)
8. Product roadmap (now/next/later)
9. GTM update
10. Hiring / org chart changes
11. People & culture pulse (eNPS, attrition)
12. Risks & mitigations
13. Decisions needed (explicit list)
14. Discussion topics
15-end. Backup data, deeper financials
```

### 4. Decisions Needed Slide

Most undervalued slide. Pre-state what you need from the board:

```
## Decisions Needed This Meeting

1. **Approve hiring plan**: 6 hires by EOQ, $720K incremental burn
2. **Greenlight enterprise pivot**: Reduce SMB investment, hire 2 AEs
3. **Approve option pool refresh**: 5% top-up for new hires

## Discussion Topics (no decision)

1. International expansion timing
2. Pricing experiment results (informational)
```

Without this slide, board meetings drift into informational mode.

### 5. Running the Meeting

```
0:00-0:05 — Welcome, agenda confirmation
0:05-0:15 — CEO update (5 min content + 10 min Q&A)
0:15-0:30 — Key metrics walkthrough
0:30-1:00 — Deep dive #1
1:00-1:30 — Deep dive #2
1:30-1:45 — Financials + runway
1:45-2:00 — Decisions
2:00-2:15 — Open discussion / executive session (no founders)
```

**Executive session matters.** Last 15 min, founders leave the room.
Board talks honestly about CEO performance, company risks. Then 1 board
member relays the discussion to CEO.

### 6. Between-Meeting Cadence

Don't go silent between meetings.

| Cadence | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Weekly | 1-line update via email or Slack to chair |
| Bi-weekly | Brief async update to full board (3-5 bullets) |
| Monthly | Investor update doubles as board update |
| As needed | Heads-up calls before bad news lands publicly |

### 7. Independent Director Recruitment

Looking for an independent director? Score candidates on:

| Dimension | Question |
|-----------|----------|
| Domain expertise | Have they operated in our market? |
| Stage relevance | Have they been at our stage AND our destination? |
| Network | Can they open doors we can't? |
| Time commitment | Will they spend 10-15 hours/quarter? |
| Diversity | What perspective do they add the rest of the board lacks? |
| Chemistry | Would I want to hear hard feedback from this person? |

**Compensation:** 0.25-1.0% equity vesting over 4 years, plus expenses.
At Series A, target 0.5%. At Series B+, target 0.25-0.5%.

### 8. Board Member Health Check (annual)

For each board member, assess:
- Are they engaged in meetings?
- Have they made intros / opened doors this year?
- Are they helpful between meetings?
- Are they aligned with strategy?
- Would I add them to my board today if they weren't already on it?

If 2+ "no" → have a direct conversation. If unfixable → ask them to step down.

## Anti-Patterns

| Pattern | Problem | Fix |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Board pack = 80 slides | Nobody reads | 15-25 slides max |
| Sending pack day-of | No pre-read | 48-72 hour rule |
| All update, no decisions | Wastes board talent | "Decisions needed" slide |
| Avoiding bad news | Loses trust | Lead with hard things |
| Founders dominate meeting | Doesn't use board | Active facilitation, ask each member to weigh in |
| No independent directors | Echo chamber | Add by Series A |
| Board comp via private chat | Excludes others | Email all, full transparency |
