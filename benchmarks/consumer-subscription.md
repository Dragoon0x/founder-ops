# Consumer Subscription Benchmarks (DTC, streaming, apps)

## Acquisition Cost (Blended CAC)

| Industry | Blended CAC |
|----------|------------|
| Streaming (video) | $25-75 |
| Audio/podcast subs | $10-40 |
| Meditation / wellness apps | $30-80 |
| Fitness apps | $40-100 |
| Dating apps (paid tier) | $25-60 |
| DTC food/snack | $30-80 |
| DTC personal care | $40-120 |
| DTC apparel | $25-90 |
| Newsletter (paid) | $15-45 |

## Retention Curves

**Subscription apps (Duolingo, Headspace, Calm):**
- Month 1: 100% (definition)
- Month 3: 50-65%
- Month 6: 35-45%
- Month 12: 20-30%
- Month 24: 12-20%

**DTC subscription boxes (Birchbox, Dollar Shave):**
- Month 1: 100%
- Month 3: 60-70%
- Month 6: 40-55%
- Month 12: 25-35%

## Payback Period (months)

| Tier | Payback |
|------|---------|
| Excellent | <6 months |
| Healthy | 6-12 |
| Concerning | 12-18 |
| Unsustainable | >18 |

**For consumer:** Most growth-stage consumer subs target 6-9 month payback.
Anything longer requires venture funding to bridge.

## Pricing

**Sweet spots by category:**
- Audio/news: $5-12/month, $50-100/year
- Productivity: $5-15/month, $50-150/year
- Streaming video: $8-20/month
- Wellness/health: $10-15/month, $70-100/year
- Dating premium: $15-40/month
- Software (consumer): $5-25/month

**Annual vs monthly:**
- Discount: 30-50% (annual is "free 4-6 months")
- Annual mix target: 40%+ of subs
- Annual subs churn at 1/3 the rate of monthly

## LTV Calculation

```
LTV = ARPU × Gross Margin × (1 / Monthly Churn)

For 5% monthly churn, $10/mo ARPU, 90% gross margin:
LTV = $10 × 0.9 × (1/0.05) = $180
```

**Healthy LTV:CAC for consumer subs: 3:1.**
Above 5:1 = under-investing in growth.

## Cancellation Patterns

- 50-70% of cancellations happen in first 60 days
- Annual subs: most cancellations happen in last 30 days of subscription
- Win-back campaigns: 5-15% recovery within 90 days
- Pause feature can save 20-40% of churners (vs forcing cancel)

## Key Levers for Improvement

In order of impact:
1. **Onboarding completion** (improves D7/D30 by 30-50%)
2. **Habit formation in first 14 days** (3 sessions in week 1 = 2x retention)
3. **Annual upgrade flow** (after 60 days)
4. **Pause vs cancel option** (saves 25-40% of churners)
5. **Win-back campaigns** (recovers 5-15% within 90 days)
