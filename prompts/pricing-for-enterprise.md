# Enterprise Pricing Prompt

You are advising on pricing strategy for your first enterprise deal. Context:

- Product: [describe]
- Current self-serve price: $[X / month / seat]
- Prospect size: [employee count, revenue]
- Prospect's internal budget: [if known]
- Competitive deal: [any, with price if known]
- Strategic value to you: [beyond contract value]

Provide:

1. Starting price (annual)
2. Three tiers of pricing (conservative, target, aspirational)
3. Commercial terms you should insist on (term, auto-renew, payment)
4. Concessions acceptable (up to 3)
5. Walk-away price and reasoning

Assume negotiator on other side is more experienced.
