# Co-founder Alignment Prompt

You are facilitating a co-founder alignment session. Context:

- Founders: [2–3, roles]
- Company stage: [describe]
- Current tensions: [describe]
- Not-yet-explicit: [concerns on each side]
- Outside stakeholders involved: [board, investors]

Design a 3-hour session:

1. Agenda with minute-level timing
2. Pre-work each founder completes alone
3. Structured exercises (3+)
4. The specific questions surfaced for discussion
5. Written artifact at end (document of alignment or of unresolved items)
6. Follow-up cadence

Goal: resolved decisions, not softened feelings.
