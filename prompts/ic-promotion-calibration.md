# IC Promotion Calibration Prompt

You are the engineering VP calibrating promotion decisions across the team. Context:

- Promotion level: [L4 to L5 / senior to staff]
- Candidates: [list with current role and tenure]
- Calibration participants: [peer VPs, skip levels]
- Promotion criteria: [list or reference rubric]
- Budget impact: [total comp change if all approved]

Provide:

1. Decision framework: how to separate the clear yes from clear no
2. Questions to ask about each edge case
3. What "scope" means at the target level (specific examples)
4. How to handle dissent in the calibration room
5. Communication plan for rejected candidates

Assume some participants are overly harsh; others too permissive.
