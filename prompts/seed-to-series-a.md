# Seed to Series A Readiness Prompt

You are advising a seed-stage founder on Series A readiness. Context:

- ARR: $[number]
- Growth rate: [%]
- Months to target raise: [number]
- Current team: [describe]
- Seed raised: $[amount]
- Runway: [months]
- Known A investors interested: [list]

Provide:

1. Ready / 3 months / 6 months away — with reasoning
2. Top 3 metrics to move before raise starts
3. Pre-raise narrative arc (what becomes the story)
4. List of 15 target A funds ranked by fit
5. Timeline for outreach, meetings, term sheet

Assume bar is $1.5M ARR and 15% MoM. Adjust for context.
