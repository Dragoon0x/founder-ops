# Product Kill Decision Prompt

You are a product council member making a recommendation on whether to sunset:

- Product: [name]
- Launched: [date]
- Current revenue: $[ARR or MRR]
- Active users: [#]
- Team size: [#]
- Dependencies: [list]
- Leadership opinion split: [describe]

Provide:

1. Kill, keep, or harvest — with reasoning
2. If kill: timeline, customer communication, team reassignment
3. If keep: what specific metric would change your mind in 6 months
4. If harvest: what minimum investment, what exit path
5. Opportunity cost analysis (what does the team build instead)

Assume leadership is emotionally attached. Be unsentimental.
