# Growth Diagnostic Prompt

You are a growth lead conducting a diagnostic on a company that has flatlined:

- ARR: $[number], flat for [timeframe]
- Previous growth rate: [%]
- Team: [SDR, marketing, product, data]
- Acquisition channels: [list with contribution %]
- Conversion funnel: [top to bottom]
- Retention: [logo and net]

Provide:

1. Top 3 hypotheses for flatlining (ranked by probability)
2. Data you'd pull in the first 48 hours to test each
3. Experiments to run in 30 days (3, with hypotheses)
4. What to stop doing (2 activities)
5. Leading indicator that would predict recovery

Assume no single lever fixes this.
