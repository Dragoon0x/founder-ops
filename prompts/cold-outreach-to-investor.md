# Prompt: Cold Outreach to Investor

## Output
`output/outreach-investor-[partner-name].md`

---

## Prompt Template

```
I want to reach out to [PARTNER NAME] at [FUND].

Why this partner specifically:
- Recent investments: [LIST 2-3 RELEVANT ONES]
- Thesis / stated focus: [FROM THEIR BIO OR WRITING]
- Why a conversation makes sense: [NON-OBVIOUS CONNECTION]
- Warm intro path (if any): [NAMES]

My company:
- One-liner: [WHAT WE DO IN 15 WORDS]
- Stage: [PRE-SEED / SEED / A]
- Traction that matters: [3-5 FACTS, NOT 20]
- Why now: [TRIGGER / INSIGHT]

What I'm asking for:
- [SPECIFIC — 15-min intro call / deck feedback / intros to X]

Run /founder-ops fundraise outreach and produce:

## PREFERRED: WARM INTRO TEMPLATE
Subject: Forwardable intro: [Company] x [Fund]
- Written so [INTRODUCER] can forward AS-IS
- 3-4 sentences total
- Includes the ask, the traction, the reason for fit
- One link (deck OR demo, not both)

## COLD VERSION: 3 DRAFTS

### Version A: Partner's Writing + Differentiation
- Open with specific reference to their recent writing/investment
- Differentiate from any portfolio conflict they'd worry about
- One-sentence value prop
- 2-3 traction facts
- Specific CTA with time window

### Version B: Insight-First
- Open with industry observation (earned, not generic)
- Explain why this insight made us build X
- Minimal pitch; maximum demonstration of pattern-matching
- CTA

### Version C: Customer Story
- Open with a specific customer win (quote if possible)
- Expand outward to category
- Our advantage in closing similar
- CTA

## RULES
- Subject under 40 chars
- Email under 150 words
- ONE ask
- ONE link
- Founders, not "the team"
- No "would love to connect" / "disrupt" / "10x" / "game-changing"
- Specific meeting time suggested (not "how's your schedule")

## ANTICIPATE + PREPARE
- Top 5 questions they'll ask (based on their portfolio pattern)
- Best 1-paragraph answer to each
- 3 references I can offer pro-actively

## FOLLOW-UP CADENCE
- No reply in 4 days → follow up once, adding new info (signed customer, launch, etc.)
- No reply in 10 days after that → move on
- No pestering

Save as output/outreach-investor-[PARTNER].md.
```
