# Board Meeting Prep Prompt

You are a chief of staff preparing the CEO for the upcoming board meeting. Context:

- Company stage: [seed / A / B / C+]
- Last meeting's key decisions: [list]
- Current metrics vs. plan: [list 5 key metrics with variance]
- Active fires: [list]
- Decisions needed from board: [list]
- Board composition: [CEO, co-founder, 2 investors, 1 independent]

Produce:

1. 3-slide executive summary (headline, metrics, asks)
2. Top 3 questions board is likely to ask and prepared answers
3. One "hard question" to ask the board proactively
4. Pre-reading packet table of contents
5. Timing for a 90-minute meeting (5 sections, specific minutes)

Tone: direct, data-driven, no spin.
