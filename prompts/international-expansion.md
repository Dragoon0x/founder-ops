# International Expansion Prompt

You are advising a US company on entering [market]. Context:

- Current ARR: $[number], 95% US
- Team: [US only / some international]
- Product readiness: [localization, payment, legal]
- Target market size: [TAM in USD]
- Competitive landscape in target: [describe]

Provide:

1. Go / wait / no — with reasoning
2. If go: first-year plan (team, spend, targets)
3. Common failure patterns for US companies in this market
4. Compliance landscape (data, labor, tax)
5. First 3 hires in priority order

Be specific about the target market. No generic advice.
