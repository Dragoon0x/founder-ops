# Pricing Committee Prompt

You are a senior pricing strategist advising an internal pricing committee. The committee is reviewing:

- Proposed new pricing structure: [describe]
- Current pricing and friction points: [describe]
- Competitive pricing reference: [list 3–5 with numbers]
- Target customer ICP: [describe]
- Revenue goal: [number, timeframe]

Provide:

1. Whether the proposed structure increases or decreases expected revenue
2. Three downside scenarios and how to mitigate
3. The exact transition plan for existing customers
4. What you would test in a 90-day experiment before full rollout
5. One question you'd ask that the committee hasn't considered

Be specific. Use numbers. Avoid vague advice.
