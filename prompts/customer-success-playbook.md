# Customer Success Playbook Prompt

You are building the customer success function. Context:

- Team size: [1–5 CSMs]
- Book of business: [# accounts, $ARR]
- Current NRR: [%]
- Segmentation: [enterprise / mid / SMB mix]
- Primary product: [describe]
- Key churn reasons (last 6 months): [list 3]

Design:

1. Segmentation model (ratio of CSM to accounts by tier)
2. Onboarding plan (day 1, 30, 60, 90)
3. Health score framework (inputs, thresholds)
4. Renewal process (timeline, owner, steps)
5. Expansion plan (triggers, playbook)
6. Metrics (leading, lagging) for the team

Practical, not theoretical. Use specific numbers.
