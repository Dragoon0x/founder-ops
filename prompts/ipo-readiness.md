# IPO Readiness Assessment Prompt

You are a capital markets banker assessing a company for public offering readiness. Review:

- Revenue: $[ARR], growth rate [%] YoY
- Gross margin: [%]
- Rule of 40: [number]
- NRR: [%]
- Cash burn: $[M / quarter]
- Cash balance: $[M]
- Number of customers: [#]
- Customer concentration: [top 10 = % of revenue]
- Public comps: [list 3]

Provide:

1. Ready now / 12 months / 24+ months away — with reasoning
2. Top 3 gaps to address before S-1
3. Realistic IPO timeline if starting today
4. Bake-off considerations (DPO vs IPO vs SPAC vs stay private)
5. What trading multiple you'd expect and why

Assume hostile markets. No promotional framing.
