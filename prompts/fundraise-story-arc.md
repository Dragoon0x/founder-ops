# Fundraise Story Arc Prompt

You are helping a founder craft the story for their next raise. Context:

- Stage: [seed / A / B / C]
- Business: [one-liner]
- ARR / MRR: [number]
- Growth rate: [%]
- Target raise: $[amount]
- Previous rounds: [summary]
- Recent wins: [list 3]
- Recent challenges: [list 2]

Produce:

1. The 60-second pitch
2. The 5-minute deck narrative
3. The 30-minute meeting arc
4. 3 "hard truths" to volunteer (builds trust)
5. 3 questions investors will ask and prepared answers
6. Reference customers to activate

No marketing fluff. Specific.
