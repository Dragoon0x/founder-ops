# Investor Update — October 2025 (Rough Month Example)

**Subject: [Anon Labs] October update: down month, what we're doing**

Hi team,

Writing this one earlier than usual because I want you to hear the numbers from me before the board meeting.

## Numbers

- **MRR:** $61K (down from $68K last month, -10%)
- **Churn:** 2 logos, total $9.3K MRR
- **Net new MRR:** -$7K (first negative month since pre-seed)
- **Runway:** 11 months at current burn ($340K/mo). 7 months if we don't cut.
- **Cash:** $3.74M

## What happened

Two of our largest accounts churned. One ($6.8K MRR) was acquired and consolidated on an internal tool. The other ($2.5K MRR) we lost to a competitor that went to market with a 40% lower price.

New bookings came in at $4.5K MRR, which is our lowest since February. Two deals we expected to close pushed to Q1.

## What I got wrong

1. I underestimated the impact of Orion's pricing change. We've been losing 4 of 7 SMB deals to them for 3 months now. I kept expecting it to stabilize. It hasn't.
2. I delayed the expansion motion (post-sale CSM/AE) for one more quarter because the numbers "looked fine." They didn't. Our cohorts have been flattening for 3 months.
3. Our onboarding rebuild took 6 weeks longer than planned, which pushed back pipeline generation.

## What we're doing

1. **Cutting burn from $340K to $260K starting Nov 1.** This extends runway to ~14 months. Details: pausing 2 open reqs, cutting ad spend by 40%, renegotiating our cloud contract (committed usage discount).
2. **Moving upmarket.** We're sunsetting the starter plan (under $500/mo) Jan 1 and raising our minimum to $1,200/mo. We'll lose 18 logos but gain focus.
3. **Building an expansion motion.** Promoting one of our AEs to Expansion Lead. Hiring 1 CSM. Starting QBRs with top 20 accounts.
4. **Acknowledging our competitive loss.** We're commissioning a win/loss study with a third party (starts next week). We need real data, not our own narrative.

## What I'm asking for

1. **Brutal feedback on our positioning.** If you've seen our pitch in the last 90 days, what doesn't land? Reply honestly.
2. **Intros to 2 specific people** (listed below) — both are VP of Expansion at companies I respect.
3. **Permission to revise our Series A plan.** I'll send a proposal next week. Short version: we delay Series A by 2 quarters, use the time to fix NRR, and raise at higher metrics instead of rushing.

## What I'm not asking for

I'm not asking for a bridge. We have runway. We have a path. We just had a bad month and need to execute.

More in the board deck.

— Founder
