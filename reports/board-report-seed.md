## What happened this month

Three things drove the MRR jump:
1. Two pilots converted to paid (Delta Corp and Osprey Inc)
2. One new logo via inbound (signed in 9 days from first call)
3. Price increase on two legacy accounts (from $500 to $900/mo)

The 9-day sales cycle is a strong signal. Inbound close rate this month was 3/7 demos. Last month was 1/5.
