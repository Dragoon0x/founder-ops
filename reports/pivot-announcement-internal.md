# Team Memo — We're Pivoting (Example)

**From:** Founder
**To:** Team (all 14 of us)
**Sent:** Monday morning, before the all-hands
**Date:** [internal]

---

Team,

I'm sending this before the all-hands so you can read it carefully. We'll discuss at 10am.

## The short version

Starting Q1, we're shutting down our current product (ShipWatch for Marketing) and launching a new one (ShipWatch for RevOps).

## Why

We spent 18 months building for marketing teams. We got to $480K ARR with 62 customers. We are not growing fast enough to be venture-backable in this market. Three specific things convinced me:

1. Our sales cycle is 70% longer than our closest comps (because marketers aren't buyers — they have to route every purchase)
2. Our churn is structural (marketing teams cut tools first in any budget pressure)
3. Our best customers are the ones who use us for RevOps use cases anyway (18 out of our top 20)

We don't have another 18 months to iterate toward product-market fit. We have 9 months of runway.

## What's changing

1. **Product.** We're keeping the underlying engine. We're rebuilding the surfaces for RevOps use cases: pipeline analytics, rep scorecards, forecasting.
2. **Customers.** We're keeping all 62 existing customers. They'll get grandfathered. About 18 of them are already using us in RevOps mode — they'll transition seamlessly. The other 44 we'll support but won't prioritize.
3. **Go-to-market.** Different ICP. Different champion. Different messaging. Different pricing.
4. **Your work.** Engineering continues, mostly. Design retargets. Sales re-qualifies pipeline. Marketing starts from scratch on content and SEO.

## What's NOT changing

1. The team. No layoffs. Every role we have is a role we need in the new direction.
2. Compensation.
3. Our values.
4. The mission: make teams better at their work with honest data.

## Timeline

- **This week:** align on the plan as a leadership team
- **Next 2 weeks:** I'll do 1:1 with each of you about how your role shifts
- **Weeks 3-8:** build the RevOps product (we have 70% of what we need)
- **Week 9:** soft launch to existing customers using us for RevOps
- **Week 12:** launch externally

## What I need from you

1. **Read this carefully.** If something doesn't make sense, ask in the all-hands or DM me.
2. **Be honest about your role.** If you joined us to work on marketing products specifically and this pivot makes you want to leave, tell me. I'll help you find your next thing. No hard feelings.
3. **Talk to customers.** Not to fix anything yet. Just listen. Especially our top 20.

## What I owe you

1. A clear plan, week by week, by Friday.
2. Transparent financial updates every 2 weeks.
3. My best thinking on why this will work — and honest acknowledgment of what could make it wrong.

## Why I'm confident

We have something rare: a technical foundation that works, a team that ships, and a real customer signal. The pivot isn't starting from zero. It's redirecting existing momentum.

We have 9 months. That's enough.

See you at 10.

— [Founder]

P.S. If you want to talk 1:1 before the all-hands, I'm in room 3 until 9:45.
