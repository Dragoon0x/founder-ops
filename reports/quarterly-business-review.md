# Q3 2025 QBR — Internal (Example)

**Audience:** Leadership team (7 people) + extended staff (18 people)
**Format:** 90-minute in-person, materials sent 48 hours ahead
**Cadence:** First week of every quarter, reviews the quarter just ended and finalizes the new quarter's OKRs

---

## 1. How Q3 went

### Goals set vs. delivered

| Q3 OKR | Target | Actual | Grade |
|---|---|---|---|
| Net new ARR | $1.2M | $1.31M | A |
| New logos | 22 | 19 | B- |
| NDR | 120% | 108% | D |
| Release PLG onboarding | GA by Sept 15 | GA Oct 2 | C+ |
| Complete SOC 2 Type I | Pass audit | In audit | B |
| Reduce time-to-value | <7 days | 6 days | A |

Overall grade: B-. Revenue delivered but the underlying motion (NDR) cracked.

### What went well (and why)

**Gross margin hit 81%.** The infra rebuild work we scoped last quarter paid off exactly as modeled. Credit: platform team.

**Time-to-value dropped to 6 days.** The onboarding rebuild worked. Adoption of the "first-value moment" (running your first report) happens in 18 hours median now, vs. 3.5 days before.

**Average deal size up 34%.** We said no to 11 SMB deals under $10K this quarter. Focus worked.

### What went poorly (and why)

**NDR dropped 10 points.** Three factors:
1. Three expected expansion deals pushed to Q4 (timing, not loss)
2. Two accounts downgraded seats (real loss)
3. Top-10 renewal came in flat (real loss — this account should have been expanding)

Root cause: we don't have a systematic expansion motion. When our best salesperson ran expansion, NDR was 125%. Since we moved him to new logos in Q2, NDR has fallen every quarter.

**S&M Magic Number fell to 0.7.** We hired 3 AEs in April expecting 3-month ramp. They're at 4.5 months average ramp and still under 60% quota. Ramp model was wrong.

**Onboarding GA slipped 2 weeks.** We hit scope creep. PM should have cut scope at the Aug 10 checkpoint and didn't. Owning this one.
