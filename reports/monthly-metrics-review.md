## What the metrics are telling us

1. **Top-line is growing but underlying motion is weakening.** Gross adds are strong (good). Retention and expansion are trending wrong (bad).
2. **Churn spiked this month.** We lost one $6.8K/mo account (Delta Corp acquisition) and one $2.5K/mo account (competitive loss). The acquisition loss was uncontrollable. The competitive loss we need to study.
3. **Expansion ARR dropped to $8K from trailing average of $12K.** No one is actively working expansion. It's happening by accident when it happens at all.

## Root-cause drill: the $2.5K churn to competitor

- Customer: mid-market B2B SaaS
- Reason given: competitor is 40% cheaper
- Reason behind the reason (from exit interview): they hadn't used us in 6 weeks
- Pattern: we don't have activation monitoring on accounts past 90 days
- Fix: shipping account health scores next sprint

## Root-cause drill: why expansion is flat

- 3 CSM team was merged into support in July. No one owns expansion.
- Our pricing model rewards expansion (seat-based + usage-based) but nobody is prompting it.
- Usage triggers exist in our analytics but aren't automated into outreach.
- Fix: expansion team launching Nov 1, automation by end of Q4.

## Decisions

1. ✅ Approved: Expansion team structure and hiring plan
2. ✅ Approved: Ship account health scores this sprint (prioritized over roadmap features)
3. ✅ Approved: Add exit interview requirement for any churn >$1K MRR
4. 🔄 Pending: Do we offer a save-desk discount for at-risk accounts? (Decision by Nov 15)

## Leading indicators we'll watch next month

1. % of accounts with a health score (target: 100% by end of month)
2. # of expansion conversations logged in CRM (target: >50)
3. Usage trend in accounts >90 days old (target: stable or rising in 80% of accounts)
