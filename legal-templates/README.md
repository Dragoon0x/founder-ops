# Legal Templates

Founder-friendly legal reference. **Not legal advice.** These are
templates and decoder references. Every actual document should be
reviewed by a real startup lawyer.

## Why this exists
Most founders spend $10K–30K in year one on legal they could have
spec'd in an afternoon. These references help you come to the lawyer
with the right questions and the right structure.
