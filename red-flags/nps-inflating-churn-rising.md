# NPS inflating while churn rises

> If you're measuring NPS through a trickle survey while your best customers are leaving, your NPS is a lie.

## What to look for

- Biased sample: only happy customers bother to respond.
- Fix: survey everyone, including through exit. And look at weighted NPS by ARR, not logo count.
- A 55 NPS from tiny accounts while your $100K accounts are churning is worse than a 35 NPS from a balanced sample.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
