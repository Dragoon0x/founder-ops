# Founder still answering every support ticket at $2M ARR

> Early, this is a feature. At $2M ARR with 10+ people, it's a bug.

## What to look for

- Founder can't scale. Team isn't learning. Customer dependency is concentrated on one person.
- Fix: document 20 common tickets with responses. Have another person handle 80% of ticket volume. Founder handles only VIP / escalations.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
