# Two executives never disagree in front of you

> If your VP Sales and VP Marketing (or any two peer execs) always agree publicly, they're probably either: (a) not talking, or (b) quietly misaligned.

## What to look for

- Healthy teams disagree visibly in leadership meetings. Then they leave aligned.
- Unhealthy teams nod in the meeting and snipe in private.
- If you never see disagreement, create space for it. Ask each one, 'Where do you and X genuinely disagree?' If they can't name anything, push harder.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
