# Unissued options drifting into bonus territory

> If your option pool is being used for existing-employee top-ups instead of new hires, you're diluting against yourself.

## What to look for

- Run a check every quarter: what % of grants in the last 6 months went to new hires vs. existing employees?
- If >25% of recent grants are to existing employees with no promotion, you're under-compensating cash and the pool is subsidizing it.
- Fix comp, not the pool.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
