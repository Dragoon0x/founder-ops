# Monthly discount approvals piling up

> If you're approving discounts 3+ times a month, your list price is wrong.

## What to look for

- Every discount is a signal that the market is telling you something about price.
- Track discount approvals by reason: competitive, size, strategic, relationship.
- If >40% of deals get discounted, raise list price and restructure discount bands.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
