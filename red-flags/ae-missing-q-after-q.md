# AE misses quota for 2 consecutive quarters

> One quarter is noise. Two quarters is signal. Three is a decision you should have made already.

## What to look for

- Ask: is this a fixable problem (skills, ICP, enablement) or a fit problem (wrong role, wrong stage)?
- Fixable problem: structured 90-day plan with specific milestones. Weekly check-ins. If no improvement, make the change.
- Fit problem: act now. Every extra month costs the company 2-3x this person's OTE in pipeline.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
