# Roadmap exists only in the founder's head

> If only the founder can describe the roadmap, only the founder can work on the roadmap.

## What to look for

- Write it down. Once per quarter, publicly.
- Include: what we're doing, what we're not doing, what would change our mind, what we'll revisit when.
- A roadmap everyone can describe is a roadmap a team can execute against.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
