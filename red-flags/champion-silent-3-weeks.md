# Champion silent for 3+ weeks

> Your contact at a customer has gone dark. This is almost always bad.

## What to look for

- Internal change: they're being laid off, reorg'd, or moved off the project.
- Political problem: your champion lost a budget fight and doesn't want to say so.
- Competitive displacement: they're piloting an alternative.
- Act immediately. Reach out to 2 other people at the account. Send a candid message to the champion directly asking if something changed.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
