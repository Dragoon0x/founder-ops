# One customer >20% of revenue

> Concentration risk. Every investor asks. Every founder underestimates it.

## What to look for

- If one customer is >20% of ARR, you have a problem, even if they love you.
- If one customer is >33%, they're running your company.
- The fix isn't to fire them. The fix is to grow everyone else faster than them.
- If they churn, what's the plan? Write it down before you need it.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
