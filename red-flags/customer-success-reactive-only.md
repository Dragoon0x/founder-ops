# Customer success team only calls accounts when churn is imminent

> Reactive CS is support. Proactive CS is revenue.

## What to look for

- Signal: CS team's calendar is full of 'save' calls, not QBRs.
- Fix: CS must do outbound to top 30 accounts monthly, independent of any problem.
- Expansion revenue comes from relationships built before the renewal conversation.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
