# 3+ consecutive hires from the same previous employer

> Strong signal of nepotism and culture-narrowing.

## What to look for

- One great hire from an ex-colleague: fine and good.
- Three+ consecutive hires from the same company: you're building a clique, not a team.
- Force yourself to source the next role outside the network.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
