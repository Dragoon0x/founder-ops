# Support ticket volume doubling without a launch

> Unannounced pattern of support increase = something broke or changed.

## What to look for

- Check: bug in production, pricing or plan change, upstream provider outage, bad release.
- Also check: new cohort of users (bad fit customers generate more tickets).
- Fix the root cause before scaling support team size.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
