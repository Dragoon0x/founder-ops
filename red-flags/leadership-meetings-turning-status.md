# Leadership meetings turning into status readouts

> When leadership meetings become round-robin status reports, the team has stopped leading.

## What to look for

- Signal: everyone talks in turn, nobody pushes back, no real decisions get made.
- Root cause: people are protecting turf, or the founder over-talks, or there's no real agenda.
- Fix: agenda by decision needed, not function. 'What do we do about X?' not 'Update from marketing.'

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
