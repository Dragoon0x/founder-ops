# Founder goes a full week without customer contact

> Possible during fundraise weeks. Dangerous as a pattern.

## What to look for

- Week 2: okay, you're busy.
- Week 3: you're starting to lose instinct.
- Week 4: you're making decisions based on stale data.
- Block 2 hours every week for direct customer contact. Non-negotiable.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
