# Investors stop asking about runway

> Counterintuitive red flag. Engaged investors ask. Disengaged ones don't.

## What to look for

- If your board stops asking about runway, burn rate, and next-round planning, they may have mentally written you off.
- Test: ask each board member for 30 min about your next 12 months. If they cancel twice, that's your answer.
- Don't wait. Start the next-round conversation yourself, even if early.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
