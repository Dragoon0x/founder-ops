# Feature shipped and nobody did a retrospective

> No retro = no learning = repeat mistakes.

## What to look for

- Every feature shipped to production should get a 30-minute retro within 2 weeks.
- Questions: did we hit the success metric? What did we get wrong in the spec? What would we do differently?
- If you can't remember what you learned from the last 3 features, your team isn't learning.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
