# Your top rep makes no internal referrals in 12 months

> Great salespeople refer great salespeople. Silence means something.

## What to look for

- Possible causes: they're interviewing elsewhere, they're unhappy, they don't rate the company enough to recommend.
- Ask them directly: 'Would you refer your best friend from your last job?' Their body language tells you more than their words.
- Fix the environment before you fix the referrals.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
