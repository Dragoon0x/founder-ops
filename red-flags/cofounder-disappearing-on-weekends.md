# Co-founder stops responding on weekends

> Not the occasional Saturday off — a pattern of absence.

## What to look for

- If a co-founder starts going dark on weekends for 3+ consecutive weeks, something is off.
- Common causes: burnout, interviewing elsewhere, family crisis, disagreement they haven't voiced.
- Address it directly: 'I've noticed you're unreachable on weekends lately. What's going on?'
- Don't confuse a boundary (healthy) with a withdrawal (signal).

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
