# Engineering velocity halving over 2 sprints

> If throughput drops 50%+ for 2 sprints, something structural changed.

## What to look for

- Common causes: tech debt hitting a wall, a recent hire dragging velocity, a hidden personnel issue, scope creep in stories.
- Ask your tech lead for a root cause before the next sprint.
- Do not try to 'motivate' harder. The problem is structural, not motivational.

## What to do

Once you've seen the signal, open a direct conversation. Don't let it linger. Red flags compound faster than founders realize.
